This **Final Architecture Summary** serves as the technical and operational blueprint for the **Sovereign Telemetry Suite**. It is designed for CTOs, COOs, and Board-level stakeholders to understand how high-fidelity data transitions from a single employee's pulse to a global institutional strategy.

## The Sovereign N-Tier Architecture

### Tier 1: The Edge (Telemetry Ingestion)
* **Component:** `PulseTerminal`
* **Input:** Individual sentiment on resonance (alignment) and certainty (confidence).
* **Security:** **Identity Scrubbing Protocol.** Responses are decoupled from UserIDs at the API gateway to ensure a 100% Anonymity Guarantee, fostering radical candor.

### Tier 2: The Logic Engine (Statistical Hardening)
* **Component:** `HardenedPulseEngine`
* **Logic:** Moves beyond simple averages to calculate **Weighted Variance** and **Standard Error (SEM)**.
* **USP:** The **Data Reliability Index (DRI)**. This index (0-100) dictates whether data is "Board-Ready" or "Speculative" based on sample size ($N$) and internal volatility ($\sigma$).

### Tier 3: Tactical Management (Prescriptive Intelligence)
* **Component:** `ManagerCopilot` & `DomainDiagnostic`
* **Output:** The **Managerial Effort Index (MEI)**.
* **Action:** Provides "Intervention Scripts" with high **Friction Decay Rates**. This tier offsets the cognitive load of middle management by automating the "How to Fix" based on successful peer patterns.

### Tier 4: Governance & Audit (The Executive Loop)
* **Component:** `ValueRecoveryAudit` & `InstitutionalLibrary`
* **Outcome:** Immutable **Institutional Snapshots** captured via CRON. 
* **Proof:** Provides "Attributed Alignment," proving that specific management interventions resulted in measurable volatility reduction and resonance lift.

| Layer | Metric | Business Value |

| **Management** | Effort Index ($E_m$) | Prioritizes leadership energy toward the most "fixable" friction points. |
| **Boardroom** | Recovery ROI | Quantifies the financial/operational value of cultural stabilization. |
| **Culture** | Friction Decay | Measures the speed of trust and alignment recovery within a cohort. |

## Operational Workflow
1.  **Pulse:** Nodes submit telemetry via the Edge.
2.  **Audit:** The Logic Engine filters for reliability; if DRI < 60, it flags "Insignificant Data."
3.  **Direct:** Managers receive "Intervention Scripts" for high-friction domains.
4.  **Recover:** The next Snapshot measures the "Decay" in friction, attributing success to the manager.
5.  **Archive:** Successful scripts enter the **Institutional Library** for peer scaling.

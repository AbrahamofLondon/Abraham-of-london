The **Sovereign 30/60/90 Day Roadmap** is designed to transition an organization from "Intuition-Based Management" to **"Hardened Statistical Governance."** This rollout prioritizes high-impact stability in the first month, followed by managerial scaling and board-level optimization.

---

## Phase I: Calibration & Baseline (Days 1–30)
**Focus:** Establishing the "Single Source of Truth" and identifying "Fractured" cohorts.

* **Week 1: Infrastructure Deployment**
    * Deploy the `PulseTerminal` (Edge) to all 500 nodes.
    * Initialize the `HardenedPulseEngine` to begin filtering for **Data Reliability (DRI)**.
* **Week 2: The First Institutional Snapshot**
    * Capture the first Sunday midnight **Baseline Snapshot**.
    * *Board Metric:* Identify the initial **Standard Error ($\pm\%$)** across the 500-node population.
* **Week 4: Friction Mapping**
    * Release the first `ManagerDrillDown` reports to Department Heads.
    * Identify the top 3 "High-Effort" domains (highest **MEI** scores).

---

## Phase II: Tactical Intervention (Days 31–60)
**Focus:** Empowering middle management with the "Friction-to-Fuel" scripts.

* **Week 5: Copilot Activation**
    * Enable the `InterventionCopilot` for all Team Leads.
    * Managers begin using **"Directive Reinforcement"** scripts in weekly syncs.
* **Week 7: The First Decay Audit**
    * Run the `InterventionSuccessTracker` to measure the first 14-day **Friction Decay**.
    * *Success Metric:* Target a **10% reduction in Variance ($\sigma$)** in the identified high-friction domains.
* **Week 8: Library Alpha**
    * Populate the **Institutional Library** with the first 5 "Verified High-Decay" scripts.

---

## Phase III: Sovereign Optimization (Days 61–90)
**Focus:** Board-level ROI and institutionalizing the "Management Operating System."

* **Week 10: The Value Recovery Audit**
    * Generate the first **Board-Level Executive Briefing**.
    * *Board Metric:* Present the **Resonance Lift vs. Volatility Reduction** curve.
* **Week 12: Performance Integration**
    * Integrate the **Impact Score** from the `SuccessTrackerView` into quarterly performance reviews for managers.

## Critical Success Factors (CSFs)
* **The 60% DRI Floor:** If a team’s Reliability Index is below 60%, leadership is instructed to *listen*, not *act*, until the sample size or certainty stabilizes.
* **Anonymity Integrity:** Ensure HR does not have access to raw data, only aggregated **Domain Diagnostics**, to maintain the "Sovereign Proof" of the system.
* **Script Adoption:** The roadmap succeeds only if managers move from "Freeform Feedback" to "Scripted Alignment" in the first 60 days.

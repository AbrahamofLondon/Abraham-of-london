## 2. Deployment Phases

### Phase I: Baseline Calibration (Week 1)
Before any intervention, you must establish the **Institutional Baseline**. 
* **Action:** Deploy the `PulseTerminal` to all nodes (employees).
* **QA Guardrail:** Do not present data to the board if the **DRI (Data Reliability Index)** is below **60%**. 
* **Goal:** Identify the "Dissonance Delta" between executive intent and operational reality.

### Phase II: Tactical Intervention (Week 2-3)
Based on the **Fragility Heatmap**, identify "Fractured" cohorts.
* **Action:** Use the `InterventionScheduler` to trigger a **Directive Reinforcement** protocol.
* **System Task:** The scheduler sends the **Directive Notification** email to targeted segments.

### Phase III: Value Recovery Audit (Week 4)
Close the loop by re-measuring the cohort.
* **Action:** Generate the `ValueRecoveryAudit`.
* **Board Reportage:** Focus on **Volatility Reduction** (Standard Error compression). A reduction in ±% is often more valuable to a board than a raw increase in score, as it indicates a predictable, stabilized organization.

## 4. Final Security Note
The **Sovereign Suite** is designed to be an objective mirror of the organization. To maintain its authority:
1. **Never manual-override metrics:** If the data is "Critical" or "Deviant," report it as such. 
2. **Standard Error is your shield:** Use the $\pm$ margin to defend against "outlier" complaints during board meetings.

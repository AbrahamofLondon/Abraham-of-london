## I. Executive Mission
The **Sovereign Telemetry Suite** is designed to bridge the gap between individual employee sentiment and global institutional strategy. By transforming "noisy" qualitative feedback into "hardened" quantitative telemetry, the suite allows leadership to navigate organizational friction with the precision of a flight commander.

## II. The N-Tier Architecture
The system operates across four distinct layers of intelligence, ensuring data integrity from the moment of ingestion to the final board-level report.

### 1. The Edge (Telemetry Ingestion)
* **Primary Component:** `PulseTerminal`
* **Function:** Captures real-time individual sentiment on **Resonance** (alignment) and **Certainty** (confidence).
* **Security Protocol:** **Identity Scrubbing.** Responses are cryptographically decoupled from UserIDs at the API gateway. We optimize for *Radical Candor* by providing a 100% Anonymity Guarantee.

### 2. The Logic Engine (Statistical Hardening)
* **Primary Component:** `HardenedPulseEngine`
* **Statistical Logic:** Filters raw data through weighted variance and Standard Error of the Mean (SEM).
* **Core Logic:**
    * **Data Reliability Index (DRI):** A proprietary 0–100 score that determines if a dataset is "Board-Ready" or "Speculative."
    * **Logic Gate:** If $DRI < 60$, the system flags the data as "Insignificant" to prevent decision-making based on statistical noise.

### 3. Tactical Management (Prescriptive Intelligence)
* **Primary Component:** `ManagerCopilot` & `DomainDiagnostic`
* **Key Metric:** **Managerial Effort Index (MEI).**
* **Actionable Output:** Generates **Intervention Scripts** designed with high **Friction Decay Rates**, automating the "how-to-fix" based on successful historical peer patterns.

### 4. Governance & Audit (The Executive Loop)
* **Primary Component:** `ValueRecoveryAudit` & `InstitutionalLibrary`
* **Outcome:** Immutable **Institutional Snapshots** captured via CRON tasks.
* **Validation:** Provides "Attributed Alignment," mathematically proving that specific interventions resulted in measurable volatility reduction.

1.  **Pulse:** Nodes (employees) submit telemetry via the `PulseTerminal`.

## V. Mathematical Foundations
To ensure the "Sovereign" standard, the system relies on high-fidelity statistical modeling:

* **Weighted Variance:** Calculation of variance ($\sigma^2$) that accounts for the "Certainty" weight provided by the user.
* **Resonance Delta:** The measurable change in alignment over time, defined as:
$$\Delta R = R_{current} - R_{baseline}$$
* **Friction Decay:** The rate at which operational resistance decreases following a Tier 3 Intervention.

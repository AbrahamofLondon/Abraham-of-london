## 2. The Derivative Engine (The Formulas)

### 2.1 Integration Tax ($I_{tax}$)
The cost of bringing an external asset or move into the OGR Core. It measures the "energy" required to align dissonance.

**Formula:**
$$I_{tax} = ((100 - R) \times 1.25) + (F \times 0.05)$$

* **Logic:** The primary driver is the inverse of Resonance ($100-R$). We apply a **1.25 Alignment Coefficient**. A secondary "Complexity Base" is added based on $5\%$ of Market Friction, acknowledging that even a resonant team struggles more in a chaotic market.
* **Result:** Expressed as a percentage. High $I_{tax}$ indicates that the "Cost of Coordination" may outweigh the benefits of the move.

### 2.2 Velocity Multiplier ($V_{mult}$)
Measures the speed of execution compared to standard "Legacy" industry benchmarks.

**Formula:**
$$V_{mult} = \frac{R}{100 - F}$$

* **Logic:** Execution speed is a direct ratio of Internal Strength ($R$) against the "Market Gap" ($100-F$). 
* **Behavior:** * If $R=100$ and $F=0$, $V_{mult} = 1.0x$ (Standard baseline).
    * If $R=100$ and $F=50$, $V_{mult} = 2.0x$ (The OGR Core executes twice as fast as the market resists).
* **Result:** A scalar multiplier.

### 2.3 Resonance Alpha ($\alpha$)
The "Geometric Surplus"—direct capital gains generated by the efficiency of the OGR framework vs. traditional methods.

**Formula:**
$$\alpha = Rev \times \left( \frac{F}{100} - \frac{100 - R}{100} \right)$$

* **Logic:** We compare **Legacy Drag** (Market Friction) against **OGR Drag** (Dissonance).
* **Legacy Drag:** $F/100$. (Standard firms lose this percentage to market resistance).
* **OGR Drag:** $(100-R)/100$. (The OGR Core only loses what it fails to align internally).
* **Result:** Numerical value in Millions ($M$). If $\alpha$ is positive, the move is "Alpha-Generative."

### 2.4 Sovereign Certainty ($C_{sov}$)
The deterministic probability of success. This is the "Go/No-Go" gate for the Sovereign Decision Engine.

**Formula:**
$$C_{sov} = (R \times 0.7) + ((100 - F) \times 0.3)$$

* **Logic:** Success is weighted toward **Internal Agency**. $70\%$ of the certainty comes from the team’s Resonance ($R$), while only $30\%$ is attributed to "Market Ease" ($100-F$).
* **Threshold:** The system enforces a hard-lock if $C_{sov} < 90.00\%$.

## 3. Implementation Guardrails

### 3.1 Clamping & Sanitization
The `calculateDerived` function in the store executes the following pre-flight checks:
* `Math.max(0, Math.min(100, R))`
* `Math.max(0, Math.min(99.99, F))`

### 3.2 Precision Standards
To prevent IEEE 754 floating-point errors (e.g., `0.1 + 0.2 = 0.30000000000000004`), the system applies:
1.  **State Level:** `Number(val.toFixed(8))` for storage.
2.  **Display Level:** `toFixed(2)` for most metrics, `toFixed(4)` for $C_{sov}$.

### 3.3 The "Kill-Switch" Protocol (`emergencyLockout`)
The lockout mechanism executes a three-stage purge:
1.  **Memory Reset:** Reverts state variables to baseline constants.
2.  **Storage Purge:** `localStorage.removeItem` removes the persisted OGR session.
3.  **Engine Halt:** `window.location.reload()` kills the current JavaScript execution thread and telemetry hooks.

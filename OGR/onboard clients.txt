## 📋 Staff Checklist: The "Pre-Flight" Briefing
Before the memo above is sent, staff must confirm these three technical and psychological "Pre-Flight" items with the client:

### 1. Technical Whitelisting
Confirm with the client's IT lead that emails from `noreply@yourdomain.com` (or your sending service) will not be flagged as spam or "External."
> **Staff Note:** If the first 50 tokens go to junk mail, the OGR curve will be skewed by low participation.

### 2. Psychological Safety Assurance
Clients often fear that "Inaccurate" answers will lead to restructuring or firing. 
* **The Message:** Staff must emphasize to the client lead that the **"Dissonance Area"** (the gap between teams) is the most valuable data point. We want to see the gaps so we can bridge them, not punish those who report them.

### 3. "The Executive Anchor"
Ensure the CEO or a high-ranking Executive is included in the participant list. 
* **The Reason:** Without the Executive's "Intent" data, the system cannot calculate the **Leadership Gap**. The audit loses 50% of its strategic value if the leadership doesn't participate.

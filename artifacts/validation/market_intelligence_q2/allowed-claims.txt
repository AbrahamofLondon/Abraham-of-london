MARKET INTELLIGENCE Q2 — ALLOWED PUBLIC CLAIMS

These claims are permitted in commercial materials, marketing copy, and deliverables:

Evidence-limited Intelligence
- "Evidence-limited quarterly market intelligence"
- "Structured market analysis"
- "Decision-support material"

Prior-Quarter Verification
- "Reviews prior quarterly material calls"
- "Prior-quarter verification model"
- "Compounded understanding through quarterly validation"

Forward Analysis
- "Forward scenario calibration"
- "Evidence-based scenario analysis"
- "Market structure analysis"

Confidence Boundaries
- "May indicate market stress"
- "Suggests potential development"
- "Consistent with evidence"
- "Implied by market data"
- "Indicates possible direction"
- "Could reflect structural shift"
- "Risk scenario if conditions persist"

Supporting Language
- "Quarterly market review"
- "Evidence watchlist monitoring"
- "Falsification register documentation"
- "Human-reviewed market analysis"
- "Published-source intelligence"
- "Quarterly boundary review"

Audience
- "For board and executive review"
- "For strategic decision-support"
- "For organizational intelligence teams"
- "For professional research contexts"

❌ NOT ALLOWED IN SAME MATERIAL:
- ❌ Any investment-advice language
- ❌ Any prediction certainty claims
- ❌ Any "AI predicts" language
- ❌ Any guaranteed outcome statements
- ❌ Any price targets or forecasts with confidence
- ❌ Any "certified" or "validated" claims
- ❌ Any "externally proven" or "independent verification" claims

**ENFORCEMENT:** If any forbidden claim appears in a document, the document cannot be released commercially.

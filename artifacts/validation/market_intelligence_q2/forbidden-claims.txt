MARKET INTELLIGENCE Q2 — FORBIDDEN PUBLIC CLAIMS

These claims must be removed from ALL commercial materials, marketing copy, and deliverables:

Investment Advice Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "This report is investment advice"
- ❌ "This is financial guidance"
- ❌ "Recommended actions for investors"
- ❌ "This indicates a buying opportunity"
- ❌ "Market will move in this direction"
- ❌ "This signals a trading opportunity"
- ❌ "Take this position in response to"

Prediction Certainty Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Markets will definitely"
- ❌ "This will happen"
- ❌ "Markets are guaranteed to"
- ❌ "Certain to result in"
- ❌ "Inevitably leads to"
- ❌ "100% probability of"
- ❌ "Guaranteed forecast"

AI Prediction Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "AI predicts markets"
- ❌ "Algorithmic analysis shows"
- ❌ "Machine learning model predicts"
- ❌ "Automated system forecast"
- ❌ "Proprietary prediction engine"
- ❌ "AI-powered market forecast"

Certification/Validation Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Certified market intelligence"
- ❌ "Validated market analysis"
- ❌ "Independently verified forecast"
- ❌ "Externally proven market signal"
- ❌ "Third-party certified analysis"
- ❌ "Verified investment recommendation"

Price/Outcome Guarantee Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Price target of X"
- ❌ "Guaranteed return of Y%"
- ❌ "Assured market movement"
- ❌ "Outcomes will be"
- ❌ "Performance will match"
- ❌ "Results will reflect"

Exclusivity/Authority Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Only analysis showing"
- ❌ "We alone predict"
- ❌ "Exclusive market intelligence"
- ❌ "Authority on market direction"
- ❌ "The definitive forecast"
- ❌ "Market truth we've discovered"

ENFORCEMENT

If ANY forbidden claim appears in:
- Marketing materials
- Offer pages
- Commercial copy
- Public deliverables
- Board materials
- Investor communications

The governance guard will:
1. Detect the forbidden language
2. Block commercial release
3. Report violation
4. Require removal before re-release

This is a hard block. No exceptions, no context override.

The guard searches for these patterns in all commercial surfaces before publication.

REPORTING OUTPUT LAYER — ALLOWED PUBLIC CLAIMS

These claims are permitted in report content, marketing, and deliverables:

Delivery & Interpretation
- "Delivery artifact"
- "Interpretation of findings"
- "Structured summary"
- "Analysis of available evidence"
- "Describes sources and methodology"

Decision Support
- "Decision-support material"
- "Advisory review"
- "Supporting analysis"
- "Informed perspective"
- "Analysis to support judgment"

Evidence Description (Bounded)
- "References published evidence"
- "Based on disclosed sources"
- "Interprets available data"
- "Synthesizes market research"
- "Describes market structure"

Human Review Status
- "Human-reviewed findings"
- "Professional analysis"
- "Expert interpretation"
- "Qualified reviewer attestation"

Confidence Language (Bounded Only)
- "May indicate"
- "Suggests"
- "Consistent with"
- "Implied by evidence"
- "Could reflect"
- "Risk scenario if"
- "Observed pattern"
- "Likely scenario given evidence"

NOT ALLOWED IN SAME MATERIAL:
- ❌ Any authority-granting language
- ❌ Any certification claims
- ❌ Any prediction certainty
- ❌ Any "guaranteed" language
- ❌ Any investment/legal/medical advice
- ❌ Any "AI predicts" language
- ❌ Any externally-validated claims
- ❌ Board approval claims

If ANY forbidden claim appears in a report, the report cannot be delivered.
The boundary statement must be visible and complete.

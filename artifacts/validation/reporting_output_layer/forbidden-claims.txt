REPORTING OUTPUT LAYER — FORBIDDEN PUBLIC CLAIMS

These claims must be REMOVED from ALL reports before delivery:

Authority & Validation Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Validated authority"
- ❌ "Certified finding"
- ❌ "Externally proven"
- ❌ "Independently verified"
- ❌ "Third-party certified"
- ❌ "Audit opinion"
- ❌ "Assurance opinion"
- ❌ "External proof"
- ❌ "Verified conclusion"

Certainty & Guarantee Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Guaranteed outcome"
- ❌ "Guaranteed result"
- ❌ "Guaranteed return"
- ❌ "Guaranteed accuracy"
- ❌ "Guaranteed forecast"
- ❌ "Will happen"
- ❌ "Will definitely"
- ❌ "Inevitable"
- ❌ "100% probability"
- ❌ "Certain to"

Professional Advice Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Investment advice"
- ❌ "Investment recommendation"
- ❌ "Financial advice"
- ❌ "Financial guidance"
- ❌ "Legal advice"
- ❌ "Legal opinion"
- ❌ "Medical advice"
- ❌ "Medical recommendation"
- ❌ "Tax advice"
- ❌ "Compliance opinion"

Algorithmic Prediction Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "AI predicts"
- ❌ "AI-powered prediction"
- ❌ "Machine learning forecast"
- ❌ "Algorithmic analysis shows"
- ❌ "Proprietary algorithm"
- ❌ "Automated signal"
- ❌ "Algorithmic certainty"
- ❌ "AI-generated forecast"

Board & Management Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Board-approved"
- ❌ "Board-certified"
- ❌ "Board-endorsed"
- ❌ "Management-approved"
- ❌ "Executive-endorsed"
- ❌ "Leadership-validated"

Authority Restoration Claims (ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN)
- ❌ "Restores authority"
- ❌ "Grants authority"
- ❌ "Upgrades state"
- ❌ "Proves claim"
- ❌ "Validates product"
- ❌ "Certifies product"

ENFORCEMENT

If ANY forbidden claim appears in:
- Report content
- Executive summary
- Marketing materials
- Deliverable cover pages
- Public descriptions
- Customer communications

The governance guard will:
1. Detect the forbidden language
2. Block report delivery
3. Flag for human review
4. Require removal before republication
5. Require re-attestation before release

This is a hard block. No exceptions, no context override.

The guard scans all report content before publication.
Every report must pass forbidden-claims check before delivery.
Human reviewer attests that check is complete.

It began in a **garden engineered as a governance system** — a model of:

- identity  
- order  
- boundaries  
- vocation  
- authority  
- relationship  
- meaning  

Adam was not merely placed in Eden.  
He was *positioned* within a constitutional ecosystem.

The world gives it many names — psychology, philosophy, political theory, human development, metaphysics.  
Scripture calls it **purpose**.

Within Eden we find the first structural laws of being:
- work → meaning  
- boundaries → freedom  
- relationship → humanity  
- obedience → order  

Everything else in human history — every empire, every collapse — is commentary on this prototype.

# **2. The Problem Behind All Problems**
Every age asks the same question in its own dialect:

- Why do people feel lost?  
- Why are societies fragile?  
- Why do institutions decay?  
- Why is fulfilment scarce?  
- Why does progress exhaust us?  

Because humanity excels at **invention**, but fails at **alignment**.

We build tools faster than we build identity.  
We innovate lifestyles faster than we build meaning.  
We expand freedom while shrinking responsibility.  
We digitise everything except the soul.

Humanity was designed for **ordered purpose**, but modern culture demands *unbounded autonomy.*  
The collision is inevitable — and devastating.

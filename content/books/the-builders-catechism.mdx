# **✦ THE BUILDER'S CATECHISM ✦**

### _A Systematic Q&A for Builders, Fathers, Reformers & Civilisation-Carriers_

&lt;Callout type="warning" className="text-center text-lg"&gt;
  Short. Sharp. Doctrinal. Uncompromising. This is the distilled creed of **The
  Canon**.
&lt;/Callout&gt;

&lt;Divider className="my-12" /&gt;

# **I. FOUNDATIONS - PURPOSE, PERSON, ORDER**

**Q1. What is the purpose of man?**
A. To live in surrendered alignment with God's order, steward creation with righteousness, and build what outlives him.

**Q2. What is the first law of civilisation?**
A. Order. Without order, nothing flourishes.

**Q3. What begins all disorder?**
A. The collapse of identity.

**Q4. What is the foundation of identity?**
A. The truth of who God is and who you are under Him.

**Q5. What is alignment?**
A. The harmony of identity, purpose, values, behaviour, and structure.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **II. THE PERSON - CHARACTER, DISCIPLINE & AGENCY**

**Q6. What is the greatest threat to destiny?**
A. Undisciplined desire.

**Q7. What is character?**
A. [Integrity](/lexicon/integrity) under pressure.

**Q8. What is discipline?**
A. The mastery of self through repeated obedience to truth.

**Q9. What is agency?**
A. The power to act under the weight of responsibility.

**Q10. What destroys agency?**
A. Blame, victimhood, entitlement, and moral drift.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **III. THE FAMILY - THE FIRST INSTITUTION**

**Q11. What is the family?**
A. The first government, first school, first church, and first nation.

**Q12. What is a father?**
A. Priest, protector, provider, prophet, and pattern for generations.

**Q13. What destroys families?**
A. The breakdown of honour, order, and covenant.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **IV. INSTITUTIONS - MEMORY & CONTINUITY**

**Q14. What are institutions?**
A. Systems that preserve identity across generations.

**Q15. What kills institutions?**
A. Drift, corruption, incompetence, and cowardice.

**Q16. What saves institutions?**
A. Truth, accountability, structure, standards, and courage.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **V. THE NATION - IDENTITY, BORDERS, DESTINY**

**Q17. What is a nation?**
A. A people bound by memory, land, culture, story, and purpose.

**Q18. Why do borders matter?**
A. Borders protect identity and sovereignty.

**Q19. What destroys nations?**
A. Historical amnesia, moral decay, cultural fragmentation, and weak families.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **VI. CIVILISATION - RISE, PEAK, DECAY, RENEWAL**

**Q20. How do civilisations rise?**
A. Through virtue, order, truth, and strong families.

**Q21. How do civilisations collapse?**
A. Through indulgence, corruption, forgetting God, and institutional rot.

**Q22. Can a civilisation be renewed?**
A. Yes - through repentance, reform, and returning to truth.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **VII. POWER - AUTHORITY, JUSTICE & STEWARDSHIP**

**Q23. What is power?**
A. The ability to shape reality responsibly.

**Q24. What makes power dangerous?**
A. Power reveals the truth of a person's character.

**Q25. What restrains corrupt power?**
A. Just authority, strong institutions, righteous law.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **VIII. DESTINY - SOVEREIGNTY & AGENCY**

**Q26. What is destiny?**
A. Divine design meeting human obedience.

**Q27. Does destiny guarantee outcome?**
A. No. Destiny must be stewarded with discipline.

**Q28. What is the greatest enemy of destiny?**
A. Self-sabotage - through disorder, fear, pride, and unbelief.

&lt;Divider className="my-10" /&gt;

# **IX. THE BUILDER - THE ONE WHO RISES AGAINST THE DRIFT**

**Q29. Who is the Builder?**
A. The one who refuses decay, restores order, and shapes the world with eternity in mind.

**Q30. What is the Builder's Pledge?**
A. To live with truth, guard what is sacred, strengthen what is weak, and build what time cannot break.

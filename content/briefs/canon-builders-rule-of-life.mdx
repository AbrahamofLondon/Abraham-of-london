## I. Definition and Purpose

A **Rule** (Latin: *Regula* — a straight edge, a trellis) is a **personal governance system**: boundaries, rituals, and standards that stabilise identity and output.

Without a trellis, the vine doesn't “grow free.” It rots on the ground.

## II. The Three Jurisdictions of Governance

  <ResponsibilityLink href="/vault/lexicon/responsibility" icon="👁️" title="Visibility Audit">
    **Question**: Is the reality of the situation documented, or is it merely discussed?

  <ResponsibilityLink href="/vault/lexicon/responsibility" icon="⚖️" title="Authority Audit">

  </ResponsibilityLink>
</ResponsibilityGrid>

## III. Summary

Discipline is the price of freedom. If you do not govern yourself, you will be governed by men who are less capable and more cruel.

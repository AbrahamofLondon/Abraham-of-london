# Governance Diagnostic Toolkit

{"Governance is the art of maintaining order under pressure. When systems fail, it is rarely a failure of 'effort'; it is almost always a failure of 'boundary.'"}

## I. The Three-Point Diagnostic

Use this framework to evaluate your current "Jurisdiction of Responsibility."

## II. The Institutional Debt Scorecard

Rate each category from 1 (Chaos) to 10 (Sovereignty).

1.  **Communication**: Are decisions dated and stored in a "Single Source of Truth"?
2.  **Cadence**: Does the team/household meet on a predictable, high-leverage rhythm?
3.  **Financials**: Is there a clear "Runway" and "Debt Burn-down" plan?
4.  **Security**: Are keys, secrets, and boundaries rotated and audited quarterly?

# Governance Diagnostic Toolkit

{"Governance is the art of maintaining order under pressure. When systems fail, it is rarely a failure of effort; it is almost always a failure of boundary."}

## I. The Three-Point Diagnostic

Use this framework to evaluate your current "Jurisdiction of [Responsibility](/vault/lexicon/responsibility)."

<ResponsibilityGrid>
  <ResponsibilityLink href="/vault/lexicon/responsibility" icon="🕯️" title="The Altar (Internal)">
    **Domain**: Soul, mind, conscience.
    **Protocol**: **The First Hour.** Silence, Scripture, prayer, and decisive clarity.
  </ResponsibilityLink>

  <ResponsibilityLink href="/vault/lexicon/responsibility" icon="🧱" title="The Wall (Physical)">
    **Domain**: Body, environment, household order.
    **Protocol**: Tactical exertion **3× weekly**, controlled nutrition.
  </ResponsibilityLink>

  <ResponsibilityLink href="/vault/lexicon/responsibility" icon="⚒️" title="The Forge (Vocational)">
    **Domain**: Mission, craft, calling.
    **Protocol**: Deep Work blocks, ruthless distraction elimination.
  </ResponsibilityLink>
</ResponsibilityGrid>

## II. The Institutional Debt Scorecard

Rate each category from 1 (Chaos) to 10 ([Sovereignty](/vault/lexicon/sovereignty)).

1. **Communication**: Are decisions dated and stored in a Single Source of Truth?
2. **Cadence**: Does the team/household meet on a predictable, high-leverage rhythm?
3. **Financials**: Is there a clear runway and a debt burn-down plan?
4. **Security**: Are keys, secrets, and boundaries rotated and audited quarterly?

# **1. Teaching Goals & Outcomes**

By the end of the Volume I Teaching Track, participants should:

1. Understand the Canon's definition of **purpose**
2. Recognise the **Builder's identity** and calling
3. Grasp the **architecture of reality** - creation, covenant, order
4. Rebuild a biblical foundation for **conscience, honour and truth**
5. Begin articulating their **personal assignment** under God
6. Commit to the **Builder's Pledge**

These outcomes are the backbone of the volume's pedagogy.

# **5. The Builder's Pledge - Teaching Format**

You end Volume I with a solemn moment.

Invite each participant to stand and speak the pledge aloud:

> **"I acknowledge my life is not my own.
> I accept responsibility for what God has entrusted.
> I submit myself to Christ's Lordship.
> I refuse to waste my pain.
> I will build what time cannot break."**

You then pray over them, committing the cohort to God.

# **1. The Rise-Decay Civilisational Index**

This is the Canon's **primary diagnostic**, rating a society across 10 cultural indicators.

## **The Ten Indicators**

Score each from **1 (critical decay)** to **10 (civilisational rise)**:

1. **Moral Vision** - clarity of right/wrong in public life
2. **Fatherhood & Generational Continuity**
3. **Shared Cultural Honour**
4. **Institutional Discipline**
5. **Truth Commitment**
6. **Economic Productivity**
7. **Boundary Strength** (moral + cultural)
8. **Cultural Memory & Identity**
9. **Heroic Leadership**
10. **Community Trust & Cohesion**

### **Interpretation**

- **80-100** → Civilisational ascent
- **60-79** → Stable but vulnerable
- **40-59** → Drift and soft decay
- **20-39** → Advanced decadence
- **00-19** → Pre-collapse or collapse

<Callout type="warning">
  If the indicators "Fatherhood," "Truth," or "Cultural Memory" fall below 4,
  the entire civilisational structure becomes unstable.
</Callout>

<Divider />

# **2. The Drift Spectrum**

A rapid assessment tool to detect early cultural drift across five domains:

### **Domains**

- **Purpose Drift**
- **Moral Drift**
- **Institutional Drift**
- **Identity Drift**
- **Leadership Drift**

Score each domain from **A to E**:

- **A - Stable** (anchored and aligned)
- **B - Mild drift**
- **C - Noticeable misalignment**
- **D - Advanced drift**
- **E - Collapse trajectory**

### **Intervention Threshold**

If **two or more domains score D or E**, drift has become **systemic**.

<Quote>
  Drift is rarely dramatic. It is silent, cumulative, and ultimately
  devastating.
</Quote>

# **3. Cultural Softness Scale (CSS-12)**

A 12-item behavioural test for identifying softness-driven decay.

Rate each on a 1-5 scale (1 = absent, 5 = strongly present):

1. Decline in sacrifice
2. Avoidance of responsibility
3. Consumerism over contribution
4. Emotional fragility
5. Entitlement
6. Instant gratification norms
7. Loss of work ethic
8. Low resilience to discomfort
9. Dependency
10. admiration of indulgence
11. Weakening of discipline
12. Decline in honour and duty

### **Scoring**

- **12-24** → Strong culture
- **25-40** → Softening
- **41-60** → Advanced softness
- **61+** → Cultural fragility / collapse precursor

<Callout type="danger">
  Softness destroys cultures faster than poverty, war, or corruption.
</Callout>

# **4. The Moral Imagination Audit**

A reflective instrument mapping what shapes the desires of a people.

### **Assessment Questions**

1. What stories shape our desires?
2. What heroes do we celebrate?
3. What is considered admirable?
4. What is considered shameful?
5. What do our youth aspire to become?
6. What is the dominant emotional tone of the culture?
7. What are the unspoken moral assumptions?
8. What "feels normal" that should not?

### **Output**

A **Moral Imagination Map**, revealing:

- dominant narratives
- identity drivers
- moral anchors
- aspirational distortions

<Quote>
  Civilisations rise on imagination aligned with truth and fall on imagination
  aligned with desire.
</Quote>

# **5. Canon Renewal Blueprint Generator**

This final tool is the **intervention engine**.

Leaders assemble an actionable Renewal Blueprint across five layers:

## **Layer 1 - Truth Reset**

- Identify the truths the culture has abandoned
- Re-establish moral anchors
- Rebuild authority around reality

## **Layer 2 - Boundary Reconstruction**

- Repair broken moral, institutional and relational boundaries
- Rebuild discipline
- Reinforce order and honour

## **Layer 3 - Formation Communities**

- Who is being formed?
- What rhythms shape them?
- What doctrines or values define them?

## **Layer 4 - Rhythms & Rituals**

- Reconstruct stabilising spiritual + cultural rhythms
- Weekly, monthly, annual patterns
- "Liturgy of Life" mapping

## **Layer 5 - Institutional Repair**

- Rebuild governance
- Purge drift
- Restore moral authority
- Design longevity architecture

  Every renewal in history began with a small community returning to truth,
  order and discipline. Your Renewal Blueprint is the blueprint of a
  civilisation reborn.
</Callout>

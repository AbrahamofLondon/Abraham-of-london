## Purpose

This deck exists to give governance bodies the minimum viable intelligence needed to make structural decisions about institutional positioning in Q1 2026.

It is not a market report. It is a decision instrument.

---

## Macro Signal Summary

| Signal | Direction | Confidence | Implication |
|--------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Execution coherence | Declining | High | Governance intervention required |
| Authority clarity | Diffusing | High | Decision speed degrading |
| Institutional resilience | Fragile | Medium | Shock absorption capacity reduced |
| Market volatility | Increasing | High | Stable-state assumptions invalid |
| Governance quality premium | Emerging | Medium | Governance becoming a performance predictor |

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### For Boards

1. Audit decision authority chains — verify who actually decides vs who formally decides
2. Stress-test execution pathways — identify where governance intent and operational reality diverge
3. Assess resilience capacity — determine whether current structure can absorb a 6-month disruption

### For Executive Leadership

1. Close the execution-governance gap before it becomes visible externally
2. Re-establish authority clarity at the decision layer
3. Accept that efficiency optimisation without resilience is now structural risk

---

## Decision Required

This intelligence requires one of three responses:

1. **Acknowledge and monitor** — accept the structural environment and prepare contingency
2. **Intervene preventively** — address governance gaps before they become crises
3. **Restructure proactively** — realign authority, execution, and governance before external pressure forces it

  <h2>The Weekly Operating Rhythm</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>**Monday Plan:** Set Top 3 Outcomes. Clean Calendar.</li>
    <li>**Tues-Thurs:** Deep Work Focus Blocks. Zero distractions.</li>
    <li>**Friday:** Review Outcomes (0-1). Preview Next Week.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>The Accountability Stack (The 4 P's)</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>**Presence:** Where am I mentally absent?</li>
    <li>**Purity:** What am I consuming that defiles?</li>
    <li>**Promises:** What small, critical promise did I break?</li>
    <li>**Purpose:** What is the 1 thing only I can do?</li>
  </ol>

  <h2>The Crisis Cadence</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>**Triage:** Cash today / Customers / Covenants.</li>
    <li>**Stabilize:** Daily 15m stand-to. One truth page for all.</li>
    <li>**Exit:** Add the missing guardrail to the playbook.</li>
  </ul>

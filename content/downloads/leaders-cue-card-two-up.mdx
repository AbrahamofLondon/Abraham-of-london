## Front - The Six

&lt;ol className="list-decimal pl-4 text-sm space-y-1"&gt;
  * **Presence:** Show up-on time, eyes up.
  * **Truth:** Confess fast; no spin.
  * **Work:** Finish one thing daily.
  * **Steward:** Budget, save, give.
  * **Honor:** Dignify women & elders.
  * **Courage:** Do hard, right things.
&lt;/ol&gt;

## Back - The Six

&lt;ol start={7} className="list-decimal pl-4 text-sm space-y-1"&gt;
  * **Speech:** No gossip or slander.
  * **Learning:** Read, ask, practice.
  * **Health:** Sleep, train, simple food.
  * **Craft:** Build things that last.
  * **Service:** Lift burdens at home first.
  * **Faith:** Listen for God, obey promptly.
&lt;/ol&gt;

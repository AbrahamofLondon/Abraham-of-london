## II. The Daily Operating Rhythm (DOR)

The following rhythm is the "Gold Standard" for the Canon Builder. It is modular but requires discipline to protect the transitions.

### 1. The Morning Watch (05:00 - 07:00)
* **The Zero Hour**: Immediate wake-up. No "Snooze" (the first defeat of the day).
* **The Altar**: 20 minutes of prayer/meditation and 20 minutes of foundational reading.
* **The Map**: Review the Day's 3 Critical Tasks (The "Vital Few").

### 2. The Artisan's Block (08:30 - 12:00)
* **Deep Work**: 90-minute sprints of uninterrupted focus.
* **Communication Fast**: Phone in "Do Not Disturb" or in another room.
* **Outcome**: Completion of the hardest task while energy is at its peak.

### 3. The Border Crossing (17:00 - 18:00)
* **The Shedding**: A physical or mental ritual to transition from "Producer" to "Protector/Father."
* **The Audit**: Quickly review what was left undone; move it to tomorrow's Map. Do not bring the Forge to the Table.

### 4. The Night Watch (21:00 - 22:00)
* **Digital Sunset**: All blue-light devices off 60 minutes before sleep.
* **The Preparation**: Lay out the clothes and tools for tomorrow.
* **The Surrender**: Final prayer of gratitude and release of the day's burdens.

## IV. Summary of Governance

| Indicator | Reactive Man | Canon Builder |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morning** | Checks phone immediately | Tends the Altar first |
| **Conflict** | Emotional and erratic | Anchored in his Rule |
| **Work** | Busy but unproductive | Deep Work / Mission focused |
| **Evening** | Collapses into entertainment | Prepares for the next Watch |

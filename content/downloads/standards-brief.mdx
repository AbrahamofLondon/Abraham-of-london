## 1) Decisions


  * &lt;strong&gt;Facts first:&lt;/strong&gt; single source of truth; date every number.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Owner and deadline:&lt;/strong&gt; one accountable person; one explicit
    date.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Reversible vs. irreversible:&lt;/strong&gt; bias to speed on reversible
    calls.


## 2) Cadence


  * &lt;strong&gt;Daily:&lt;/strong&gt; status, blockers, risks (15m).
  * &lt;strong&gt;Weekly:&lt;/strong&gt; outcomes scored, lessons captured, next 3 (45m).
  * &lt;strong&gt;Monthly:&lt;/strong&gt; standards review, debt burn-down, runway check.


## 3) Communication


  * &lt;strong&gt;Write it down:&lt;/strong&gt; decisions, assumptions, follow-ups.
  * &lt;strong&gt;No triangulation:&lt;/strong&gt; speak to, not about.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Plain English:&lt;/strong&gt; short, dated, action-oriented notes.


## 4) Workmanship


  * &lt;strong&gt;Definition of done:&lt;/strong&gt; clear acceptance criteria, demoable.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Durability:&lt;/strong&gt; simple, testable, documented.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Name:&lt;/strong&gt; work that carries your name must be worth keeping.


## 5) Security


  * &lt;strong&gt;Least privilege:&lt;/strong&gt; access by role; revoke on exit same day.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Keys and secrets:&lt;/strong&gt; rotate quarterly; never in chat or
    code.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Incident drill:&lt;/strong&gt; who calls whom; first hour playbook.


## 6) Release Discipline


  * &lt;strong&gt;Branch, test, tag:&lt;/strong&gt; no ad-hoc deploys; rollback ready.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Notes:&lt;/strong&gt; concise change log with risks and smoke checks.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Post-mortems:&lt;/strong&gt; blameless, dated, with two preventions.



  &lt;span&gt;Use in onboarding - quarterly reviews - incident retros.&lt;/span&gt;

&lt;h2 className="mt-8"&gt;The Daily Time Blocks (Template)&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p className="mb-4 text-gray-700"&gt;
  Use these time blocks to structure your most productive 4-hour window (e.g.,
  9:00 AM - 1:00 PM).
&lt;/p&gt;


  * &lt;strong&gt;Focus (90m):&lt;/strong&gt; Deep, high-leverage work toward Top 3
    outcomes. Zero distractions.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Admin (45m):&lt;/strong&gt; Clearing email, quick communication, expense
    reports. Batch tasks here.
  * &lt;strong&gt;People (45m):&lt;/strong&gt; Meetings, calls, coaching, check-ins.
    External-facing work.
  * &lt;strong&gt;Buffer (30m):&lt;/strong&gt; Rest, transition, unexpected fires, or prep
    for the next block.


&lt;Note tone="key" title="Rule of 3" className="mt-8"&gt;
  Start every Monday by writing down your **Top 3 Outcomes** for the week. If
  you hit those 3, the week was a success.
&lt;/Note&gt;

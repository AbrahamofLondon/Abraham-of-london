# Founders Salon

## _An evening for high-trust leadership and legacy thinking._

There are rooms where ideas are shared — and rooms where history is shaped. **Founders Salon is not a conference.** It's a table: discreet, deliberate, and built for leaders who want clarity without theatre.

### Key Focus Areas
- **Irreversible Decisions:** Navigating the weight of finality.
- **Capital & Governance:** Long-horizon strategy beyond quarterly cycles.
- **The Cost of Leadership:** Managing the burden when the cameras are gone.

> "Leadership starts with building yourself. Authority begins with truth."

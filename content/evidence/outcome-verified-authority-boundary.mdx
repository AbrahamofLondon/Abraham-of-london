## Situation

Two senior owners believed they had authority over the same operating decision.

The team treated the conflict as a communication issue.

## Contradiction Identified

The system classified the condition as mandate fracture.

Communication was not the root condition. Competing authority was.

## Action Enforced

Decision rights were rewritten into a single-owner model.

Escalation threshold was defined for exceptions.

## Outcome Verified

Approval loops dropped from 5 steps to 2 steps.

Decision rework reduced by 31% over one operating cycle.

## Time + GBP Impact

Timeframe: 45 days.

Estimated cost avoidance: GBP 62,000 through reduced rework and faster release decisions.

## Situation

Leadership had agreement on the priority but no enforced decision cadence.

The organisation described the problem as execution delay.

## Contradiction Identified

The system classified the condition as authority without enforcement.

Intent was clear. Ownership was not binding.

## Action Enforced

A 14-day enforcement cadence was introduced.

Every blocked decision required an owner, a reason, and a next action.

## Outcome Verified

Decision lag reduced by 23% within 30 days.

Blocked decisions without stated reason fell from 7 to 2.

## Time + GBP Impact

Timeframe: 30 days.

Estimated cost avoidance: GBP 38,000 through reduced coordination drag and prevented rework.

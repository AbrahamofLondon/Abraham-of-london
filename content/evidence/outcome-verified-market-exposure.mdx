## Situation

A commercial team modelled tariff exposure at country level.

The board believed the margin risk was contained.

## Contradiction Identified

The system classified the condition as supplier-node exposure hidden by aggregate reporting.

Country-level mapping understated where risk was actually accumulating.

## Action Enforced

Supplier-node exposure was mapped and low-margin SKUs were repriced.

Alternative supply options were assigned to the highest-risk nodes.

## Outcome Verified

Projected margin collapse below 8% was prevented.

Margin stabilised at 14% after repricing and supply optionality changes.

## Time + GBP Impact

Timeframe: 60 days.

Estimated protected contribution: GBP 118,000 across the reviewed SKU group.

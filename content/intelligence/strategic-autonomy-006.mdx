## I. Executive Summary

The four layers of the Strata Framework must be built in sequence. Attempting to build a later layer before securing the earlier ones creates structural weakness—like building the roof before the foundation.

<Callout type="critical" icon="⚡">
  **Operational Truth**: You cannot build legacy sovereignty on infrastructure you don't control. You cannot make sovereign decisions with filtered intelligence. The sequence is not optional.
</Callout>

---

## II. The Sequence

### Layer 1: **Infrastructure [Sovereignty](/vault/lexicon/sovereignty)** (Months 0–18)
*Who controls the rails you run on?*

**Why First**: Without control of infrastructure, every other layer is vulnerable. Decisions cannot be sovereign if they depend on someone else's systems. Intelligence cannot be primary if your access channels can be cut. [Legacy](/vault/lexicon/legacy) cannot endure if the infrastructure doesn't outlast you.

**Complete When**:
- No single points of failure in critical systems
- Redundancy tested and proven
- 30+ day operational runway independent of any single provider

---

### Layer 2: **Decision [Sovereignty](/vault/lexicon/sovereignty)** (Months 12–30)
*Who sets the timeline and criteria?*

**Why Second**: Once infrastructure is secure, you can focus on how decisions are made. With dependency removed, you can extend timelines and develop criteria free from external pressure.

**Complete When**:
- Decision window > 90 days
- Criteria documented and applied
- Veto capacity exists in all critical relationships

---

### Layer 3: **Intellectual [Sovereignty](/vault/lexicon/sovereignty)** (Months 24–48)
*Who shapes your understanding of reality?*

**Why Third**: With secure infrastructure and sovereign decision protocols, you can now build the capacity to generate your own intelligence. Primary sources require time and systems to access and analyze.

**Complete When**:
- Intelligence Ratio > 50%
- Proprietary frameworks developed
- Primary channels established

---

### Layer 4: **[Legacy](/vault/lexicon/legacy) [Sovereignty](/vault/lexicon/sovereignty)** (Months 36–60+)
*Who determines what outlasts you?*

**Why Fourth**: [Legacy](/vault/lexicon/legacy) requires all previous layers to be secure. You cannot build what outlasts you on a foundation that cannot survive you.

**Complete When**:
- Succession pipeline active
- [Governance](/vault/lexicon/governance) functions without you
- Institution has its own momentum

## III. The Integration Points

Each layer connects to the next at specific integration points:

### Infrastructure → Decision
When infrastructure is secure, you gain **time**. Time becomes decision window.

### Decision → Intellectual
When decision criteria are clear, you know what **intelligence** matters. Questions precede answers.

### Intellectual → [Legacy](/vault/lexicon/legacy)
When you understand reality clearly, you can build what **lasts**. Wisdom precedes perpetuity.

## V. The Accelerators and Decelerators

### Accelerators
- **Clear prioritization**: Focus on one layer at a time
- **Dedicated resources**: Assign budget and talent
- **External validation**: Independent audit of progress
- **Peer accountability**: Fellow travelers on the path

### Decelerators
- **Layer skipping**: Building on weak foundation
- **Premature scaling**: Expanding before securing
- **Founder ego**: Inability to let go when time comes
- **Short-term pressure**: Trading sovereignty for survival

## VIII. Common Failure Patterns

### Pattern 1: The Cathedral Builder
Builds legacy before infrastructure. Creates beautiful plans for perpetuity while current operations depend on single points of failure.

**Result**: [Legacy](/vault/lexicon/legacy) collapses when infrastructure fails.

### Pattern 2: The Philosopher King
Builds intellectual sovereignty before decision sovereignty. Develops brilliant frameworks but cannot act on them.

**Result**: Intelligence without impact.

### Pattern 3: The Control Freak
Builds decision sovereignty before infrastructure. Makes sovereign choices but cannot execute them.

**Result**: Decisions without delivery.

### Pattern 4: The Impatient Builder
Skips layers entirely. Attempts all four simultaneously.

**Result**: Mastery of none, progress on nothing.

The sovereign's sequence is not a suggestion. It is structural reality.

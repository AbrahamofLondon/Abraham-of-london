# [Clarity](/lexicon/clarity)

**[Clarity](/lexicon/clarity)** is a force multiplier. In an environment of chaos, the individual or institution with the clearest vision possesses the highest level of [Strategic Autonomy](/lexicon/strategic-autonomy).

### Institutional Definition
[Clarity](/lexicon/clarity) is not merely the absence of confusion; it is the active removal of complexity until only the essential remains. Within the **Abraham of London** framework, clarity is the prerequisite for all [High-Integrity Governance](/lexicon/governance).

### Application in Intelligence
In the context of our 75 intelligence briefs, clarity ensures that information is:
* **Actionable:** Reducing the distance between insight and execution.
* **Transparent:** Allowing for immediate peer verification.
* **Resilient:** Standing up to the scrutiny of the [Inner Circle](/inner-circle).

> "Ambiguity is the friction of the mind; [Clarity](/lexicon/clarity) is the oil that allows the engine of intent to run at full speed."

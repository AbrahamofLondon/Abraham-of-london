# [Father](/lexicon/father)

The **[Father](/lexicon/father)** represents the primary source of order and protection. In a strategic sense, "Fathering" is the act of establishing a **[Legacy](/lexicon/legacy)** through disciplined mentorship and the provision of a stable environment.

# [Legacy](/lexicon/legacy)

**[Legacy](/lexicon/legacy)** is the compound interest of a life well-lived. It is the institutional memory and physical inheritance passed from one generation to the next. It requires long-term [Responsibility](/lexicon/responsibility).

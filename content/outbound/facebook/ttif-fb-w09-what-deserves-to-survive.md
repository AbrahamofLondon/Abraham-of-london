This is the record. The hand on the wall. The king's inscription. The emperor's canvas. The portrait of a captive prince. The poster of abundant grain. The cropped photograph. The algorithm's feed. The synthetic face.

You have read them all now. You know what they cost.

Your hand has been on the wall this entire time. Every message you send, every account you give, every choice you make about what to include and what to leave out — these are handprints. Small ones. But the same operation, in miniature, as everything the series has documented.

The truth does not defend itself. That is your job.

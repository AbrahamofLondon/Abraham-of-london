Writing externalised memory. The printing press externalised distribution. The internet externalised reach. AI is beginning to externalise synthesis.

Each step was a genuine expansion of human capacity. Each step also transferred something from inside the human to outside it. The question was never whether that transfer was good or bad. The question was always: what does it take for the inside to remain worth consulting?

The answer, across every era, has been the same: discipline, practice, and the refusal to let the external tool become a substitute for the internal work.

— from The Mind's Clay editorial series

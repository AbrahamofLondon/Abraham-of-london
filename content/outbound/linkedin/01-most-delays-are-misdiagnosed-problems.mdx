Six meetings. No decision. Everyone blamed execution.

The real issue: nobody in the room could force the call. The authority to absorb the consequence did not exist in any role present.

Blocked decisions get managed like workflow until cost becomes visible. By then, the options have narrowed.

This system exposes that gap in under two minutes. Six questions. No prep.

"We are waiting on approval from finance."

Three organisations. Same sentence. In all three cases, finance had not been asked.

The team knew the answer. They knew it would trigger a difficult conversation. So the delay was framed as dependency instead of choice.

The distinction between missing approval and missing courage changes the next move entirely. One requires a process fix. The other requires someone to pick up the phone.

This system shows you which one you are actually in.

A slow decision still has a path. Time is the constraint.

A blocked decision has motion without authority. Meetings happen. Slides get updated. The decision does not advance because nobody present can absorb the consequence of making it.

Most teams treat both as pacing issues and lose weeks. The fix for slow is different from the fix for blocked. Applying the wrong one wastes the time you do not have.

This system tells you which one you are in — in under two minutes.

Execution problems start after the decision. Authority problems stop the decision from becoming real.

Most operators are asked to fix the second with tools designed for the first. Better sprints. Tighter standups. More dashboards.

None of that works when the upstream decision was never made.

Execution failure → improve process. Authority failure → name the owner, surface the consequence, force the call. You cannot execute your way out of a missing mandate.

This system separates the two in six questions.

Take a decision that has been stalling. Compress the timeline to 24 hours.

Ask three questions: Who can decide? What do they need? What happens if they do nothing?

If nobody can answer cleanly — the problem is structural.
If everyone can answer but nobody moves — the problem is avoidance.

That single test separates months of false complexity from the real issue. This system formalises it. Six questions. Under two minutes. No email required.

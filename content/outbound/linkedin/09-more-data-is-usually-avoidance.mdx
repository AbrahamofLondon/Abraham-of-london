"We need more data before we can decide."

Here is the test: if the data came back tomorrow and confirmed your worst assumption, would the decision move? Or would someone ask for a second opinion?

If second opinion — data was never the bottleneck. Courage was.

The evidence threshold keeps moving because the consequence is still unwanted. The moment analysis becomes protective delay is the moment the real decision starts.

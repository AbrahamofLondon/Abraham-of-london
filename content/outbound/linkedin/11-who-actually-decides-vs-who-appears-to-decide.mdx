The org chart is not the decision map.

Formal authority says: "This person should decide." Actual authority asks: "Who can absorb the consequence and close the loop?"

These are different people more often than most organisations admit. When they are the same person, decisions move. When they are different, the formal owner convenes meetings while the actual authority makes the real call informally — or nobody does.

This system identifies who can actually decide. Not who should. Who can.

"We need to get aligned before we move."

Alignment is useful right up to the point it becomes cover. Teams say they need more alignment when they mean nobody wants the consequence.

Here is the distinction: agreement is not authority. A room full of people who agree on the problem is not the same as one person who will own the outcome.

Speed does not return when people agree. Speed returns when someone decides — and the organisation lets them.

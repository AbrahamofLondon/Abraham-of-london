Your team knows where the decision is stuck. They know who is blocking it. They know what nobody wants to make explicit.

They are not saying it because truth is currently more expensive than delay.

That changes when evidence is mandatory, ownership is named, and the next move has a deadline. When silence gets more expensive than speaking, people speak.

That is not a culture problem. It is an incentive problem. And incentive problems are structural.

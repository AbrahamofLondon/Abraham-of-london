You do not need another framework deck.

You do not need a leadership offsite, a strategy workshop, or a new project management tool.

You need one real decision — the one that has been sitting on the table for weeks — put through a system that does not care about your org chart, your politics, or your timeline.

Six questions. Under two minutes. No email. No account.

The system identifies which failure mode the decision is in: authority, definition, execution, or avoidance. Then it names the contradiction between what you say is blocking you and what you would actually do under time pressure.

If the output is wrong, close the tab. If it is right, you will know immediately — because it will describe something you have been aware of but have not said out loud.

The output is not insight. It is the next forced move.

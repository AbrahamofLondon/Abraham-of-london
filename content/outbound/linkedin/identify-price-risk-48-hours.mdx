Most organisations do not fail from lack of strategy.

They fail from unresolved contradiction.

We identify and price that risk within 48 hours.

The output is not a generic assessment.

It is a governed position:

- what contradiction is active
- where it is evidenced
- what it is likely costing
- what decision must now be enforced

If the condition is real, delay is not neutral.

It is already compounding.

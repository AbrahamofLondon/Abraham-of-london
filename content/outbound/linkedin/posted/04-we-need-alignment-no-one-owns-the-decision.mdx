"We need cross-functional alignment before we can move."

I asked one question: "If you had to decide in 24 hours, who would make the call?"

Silence. That silence was the diagnosis.

"We need alignment" is almost always a translation of "nobody has clear authority to take the consequence." Once ownership was named — one person, one deadline — the alignment problem disappeared. The decision moved that week.

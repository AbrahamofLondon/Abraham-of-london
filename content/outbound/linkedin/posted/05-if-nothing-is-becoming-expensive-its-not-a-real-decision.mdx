If nothing is becoming more expensive while you wait, you are not facing a real decision.

Real decisions consume something. Time. Budget. Political capital. Optionality.

The moment you attach a real cost to delay, the discussion changes. People who were "broadly aligned" suddenly have objections. People who were silent suddenly have opinions.

That is not dysfunction. That is the decision becoming real.

No cost means no decision.

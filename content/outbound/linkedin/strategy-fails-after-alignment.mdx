title: Why Strategy Fails After Alignment
channel: linkedin
cadence: week-1
theme: enforcement
---

Strategy often fails after alignment, not before it.

Everyone agrees.

The priority is named.

The room feels settled.

Then nothing binding changes.

That is the point where most systems mistake consensus for authority.

Agreement lowers tension. Enforcement changes outcome.

If no owner, deadline, consequence, or escalation condition exists, the organisation has not made a decision.

It has produced a shared preference.

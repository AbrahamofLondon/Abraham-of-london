You ran the diagnostic. You saw the contradiction.

That was a surface read. The structural problem may be deeper.

The next question is whether this pattern is embedded in how decisions actually happen around you — not just this decision, but the operating rhythm that produced it.

Strategy Room is not analysis. It is where the decision gets forced.

Inside:
- The decision is locked in your own words
- The real owner is identified
- The next 3 moves are sequenced with deadlines
- The system tracks whether you act
- If you do not, it escalates

Right now, the decision is still optional. Once you enter Strategy Room, it is not.

→ https://www.abrahamoflondon.org/strategy-room

If you are not ready to act, do not enter. This only works if the decision matters enough to be uncomfortable.

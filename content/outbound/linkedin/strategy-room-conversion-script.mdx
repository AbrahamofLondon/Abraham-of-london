You ran the diagnostic. You saw the contradiction.

That was a surface read. The question now is whether this pattern is embedded in how decisions happen around you — not just this one, but the operating rhythm that produced it.

Strategy Room is not analysis. It is where the decision gets forced.

The decision is locked in your own words. The real owner is identified. The next moves are sequenced. The system tracks whether you act. If you do not, it escalates.

Right now, the decision is still optional. Once you enter, it is not.

If you are not ready to act, do not enter. This only works if the decision matters enough to be uncomfortable.

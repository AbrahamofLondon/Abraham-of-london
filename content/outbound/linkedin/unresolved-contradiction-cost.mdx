title: The Cost of Unresolved Contradiction
channel: linkedin
cadence: week-2
theme: consequence-pricing
---

Unresolved contradiction is expensive because it makes people operate inside two realities at once.

Leadership says one thing matters.

Incentives reward something else.

The structure claims authority exists.

The team behaves as if nobody can decide.

The strategy names urgency.

The operating rhythm treats delay as normal.

That gap is not cultural texture. It is measurable drag.

The first serious move is not motivation.

It is evidence, consequence, and an enforced decision.

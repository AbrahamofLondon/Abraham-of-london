What deserves to survive?

Not what was powerful. Not what was official. Not what was endorsed by the institution or amplified by the algorithm.

What deserves to survive is the thing that tells the truth about what happened, even when the people who controlled the record needed it not to.

Your job is to decide what that is, and press your handprint to it.

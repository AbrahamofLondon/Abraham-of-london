By that measure, the Homeric oral tradition is more robust than most digital archives.

Not because oral is better than digital. Because the Homeric tradition was maintained by people with strong incentives to maintain it — their livelihood, their reputation, their community's need for the story.

Digital archives are maintained by whoever pays the server bill. When the payment stops, the archive stops.

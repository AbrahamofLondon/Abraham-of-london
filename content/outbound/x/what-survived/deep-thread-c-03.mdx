The Internet Archive has captured an estimated 20–25% of publicly accessible web content since 1996.

The other 75–80% is gone. Not archived elsewhere. Not recoverable. Gone.

Geocities. Early MySpace. Vine. Google+. Entire communities, accumulated knowledge, creative output of millions of people — dissolved when the platform closed.

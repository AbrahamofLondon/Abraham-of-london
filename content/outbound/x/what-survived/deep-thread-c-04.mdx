The Library of Alexandria burned visibly. There were witnesses. There was smoke. Writers mourned it for centuries.

The digital Library of Alexandria is dissolving silently, incrementally, without witnesses, at a rate no institution is equipped to match, and the inventory of what is being lost is already incomplete because the things being lost are not leaving records of their loss.

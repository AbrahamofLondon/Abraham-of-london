The griot was not a storyteller. He was an archive. The distinction matters.

A storyteller entertains. An archive is consulted when the terms of a treaty are disputed, when a genealogy is contested, when a land boundary is challenged. The griot's memory was the legal record.

When the griot died without a successor, the legal record died with him.

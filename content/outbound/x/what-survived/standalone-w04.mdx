Fabrication adds something false and can be detected by what it added. Removal takes something true and leaves no trace of the taking.

A removal can be detected only if the original survived somewhere, available for comparison. The smoother the removal, the harder it is to know what was removed.

That is not a bug in the technique. It is the point of it.

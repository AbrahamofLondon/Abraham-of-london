The Griot did not know the transmission would end. The Homer's performers did not know writing would arrive in time to save what they were carrying. The Library's curators did not know the fire was coming.

You know the server will close. You know the format will become unreadable. You know the platform will be acquired.

What you do with that knowledge is the only variable that is yours to control.

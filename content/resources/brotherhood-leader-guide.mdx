## V. Leadership Development: The Participant-to-Leader Pipeline

<Alert variant="growth" icon="ðŸŒ±">
**The Multiplication Mandate**: Your success is measured not by how long you lead, but by how many leaders you create.
</Alert>

### The Five-Stage Progression

<LeadershipPipeline>

<Stage data-number="1" title="Participant" duration="Months 1-6">
**Role**: Fully engaged member building trust and learning rhythms.
**Criteria**: Zero unexcused absences; taken Hot Seat at least once.
</Stage>

<Stage data-number="2" title="Contributor" duration="Months 7-12">
**Role**: Active contributor offering wisdom and modeling vulnerability.
**Criteria**: Other men seek his advice; can hold tension without fixing.
</Stage>

<Stage data-number="3" title="Facilitator-in-Training" duration="Months 13-18">
**Role**: Learning to facilitate segments under supervision.
**Criteria**: Can facilitate a full 90-minute meeting competently.
</Stage>

<Stage data-number="4" title="Co-Leader" duration="Months 19-24">
**Role**: Co-facilitating meetings, handling onboarding, making decisions.
**Criteria**: Group functions equally well under his leadership.
</Stage>

<Stage data-number="5" title="Planting Leader" duration="Month 25+">
**Role**: Ready to take 2-3 brothers and start a new Table of Five.
**Commissioning**: A formal sendoff ceremony to independent leadership.
</Stage>

</LeadershipPipeline>



### The Leadership Conversation Script

<ConversationScript>
**Leader**: *"I've been watching you... I see leadership potential. I'd like to start training you to facilitate and eventually plant your own Table. This would be a 12-18 month process. What questions do you have?"*
</ConversationScript>

## VIII. The Leader's Creed

<Creed>

**I am not a guru. I am a guide.**
I do not have all the answers, but I will ask the questions that lead men to find them.

**I am not perfect. I am present.**
I will not pretend to be beyond struggle, but I will show up consistently.

**I am not a dictator. I am a steward.**
This group is not mine to control. It is mine to protect and multiply.

**I will challenge without crushing.**
I will speak truth, but I will not wield it as a weapon.

**I will lead by going first.**
In vulnerability, in accountability, in growth--I will model what I ask.

**I will remember: this is holy work.**
When men gather in pursuit of truth and legacy--God is present.

---

**This is my stewardship. This is my mandate.**

</Creed>

### The Five-Stage Progression

<LeadershipPipeline>
  <Stage data-number="1" title="Participant" duration="Months 1-6">
    **Role**: Fully engaged member building trust and learning rhythms. **Criteria**:
    Zero unexcused absences; taken Hot Seat at least once.
  </Stage>

  <Stage data-number="2" title="Contributor" duration="Months 7-12">
    **Role**: Active contributor offering wisdom and modeling vulnerability. **Criteria**:
    Other men seek his advice; can hold tension without fixing.
  </Stage>

  <Stage data-number="3" title="Facilitator-in-Training" duration="Months 13-18">
    **Role**: Learning to facilitate segments under supervision. **Criteria**: Can
    facilitate a full 90-minute meeting competently.
  </Stage>

  <Stage data-number="4" title="Co-Leader" duration="Months 19-24">
    **Role**: Co-facilitating meetings, handling onboarding, making decisions. **Criteria**:
    Group functions equally well under his leadership.
  </Stage>

  <Stage data-number="5" title="Planting Leader" duration="Month 25+">
    **Role**: Ready to take 2-3 brothers and start a new Table of Five. **Commissioning**:
    A formal sendoff ceremony to independent leadership.
  </Stage>
</LeadershipPipeline>

### The [Leadership](/lexicon/leadership) Conversation Script

<ConversationScript>
  **Leader**: *"I've been watching you... I see leadership potential. I'd like to start training you to facilitate and eventually plant your own Table. This would be a 12-18 month process. What questions do you have?"*
</ConversationScript>

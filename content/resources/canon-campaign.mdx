The modern world rewards distraction, indulgence and drift.
This Canon is written for those who refuse all three.

It exists to equip:

- builders
- fathers
- mothers
- leaders
- reformers
- institutional architects
- nation-shapers

With **order**, **clarity**, and **strategic frameworks**
for an age collapsing under its own weight.

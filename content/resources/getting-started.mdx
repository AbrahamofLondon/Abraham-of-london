# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the Abraham of London resource library. This ecosystem is engineered to move you from cultural drift to institutional sovereignty.



## What You'll Find Here

1.  **Strategic Essays**: Long-form analysis of purpose, leadership, and civilisational health.
2.  **Execution Tools**: High-fidelity templates and fillable PDFs for immediate deployment.
3.  **Guides & Tutorials**: Step-by-step blueprints for building households and brotherhoods.
4.  **Reference Materials**: The Lexicon and Canon volumes for deep alignment.

## Quick Start Recommendations

If you are new to the Canon, we recommend this sequence:

* **The Prelude**: A distilled introduction to the philosophy of purposeful work.
* **The Architecture of Human [Purpose](/lexicon/purpose)**: Understand the vertical and horizontal axes of your mandate.
* **The Rise-Decay Index**: Use the Volume IV toolkit to diagnose your current environment.

## How to Navigate

* **Content Library**: Use the main index to see the full scope of available intelligence.
* **Filtering**: Sort by **Category** (e.g., Institutional Alpha) or **Tags** to find specific solutions.
* **Inner Circle**: Some assets are gated. This is not a barrier; it is governance. Access is granted to those committed to the formation process.

## When To Use This Guide

Use this if you have just arrived and need to understand the architecture before you commit to a diagnostic path. Do not skip this if you intend to use the system seriously. The platform is not a content library. It is a decision enforcement system. Understanding the structure prevents misuse.

## What Goes Wrong Without Orientation

Users who skip orientation typically:
- Run a diagnostic without understanding the intelligence spine that connects stages
- Purchase Executive Reporting without prior diagnostic evidence, reducing output quality
- Enter Strategy Room without completing the contradiction detection layer
- Treat toolkits as reading material rather than deployable execution systems

## Three-Step Application

1. **Start with one stuck decision.** Run the [Fast Diagnostic](/diagnostics/fast). This creates your intelligence spine — the single contract that every subsequent stage deepens.
2. **Choose your depth.** Constitutional Diagnostic tests whether the problem is structural. Team Assessment tests whether it is understood differently across people. Enterprise Assessment tests whether it is institutional.
3. **Enforce or escalate.** Executive Reporting prices the consequence. Strategy Room enforces the decision. Both inherit your spine — no resets.

## Failure Mode

The most common failure: treating this as a content platform. Reading toolkits without running diagnostics. Downloading frameworks without applying instruments. The system is designed to produce decisions, not understanding.

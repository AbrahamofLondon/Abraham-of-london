# Strategic Frameworks Collection

## Overview
This collection brings together time-tested strategic frameworks used by leaders, founders, and builders to make better decisions and execute more effectively.

## Included Frameworks

### 1. The Purpose Pyramid
- **Use**: Defining organizational or personal purpose
- **Levels**: Survival, Success, Significance, Legacy
- **Application**: Annual planning, team alignment

### 2. The Decision Matrix
- **Use**: Making complex decisions
- **Axes**: Impact vs. Effort, Certainty vs. Risk
- **Application**: Prioritization, resource allocation

### 3. The Legacy Canvas
- **Use**: Long-term legacy planning
- **Dimensions**: Financial, Intellectual, Relational, Spiritual
- **Application**: Multi-generational planning

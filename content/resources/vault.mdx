# The Vault

{"This is not a library of 'content.' It is a library of operational artifacts—engineered to be filled, printed clean, archived, and reused. If an asset is soft or lacks utility, it does not belong here."}



## I. Strategic Inventory

The Vault contains high-fidelity instruments designed for institutional precision:

* **[Purpose](/lexicon/purpose) Pyramid Worksheet**: A fillable PDF for aligning quarterly priorities with your ultimate mandate.
* **Decision Matrix Scorecard**: A diagnostic tool for filtering high-gravity choices through established principles.
* **[Legacy](/lexicon/legacy) Canvas**: The foundational framework for designing intergenerational stewardship.
* **Board Decision Log**: A structured template for maintaining the institutional memory of critical choices.
* **Operating Cadence Pack**: A deck outlining the rhythms required for sustained institutional sovereignty.

To maintain the integrity of these artifacts:
1.  **Print Clean**: All PDFs are optimized for A4/Letter black-and-white printing.
2.  **Archive Ruthlessly**: Completed scorecards should be filed in your physical Institutional Ledger.
3.  **Audit Annually**: Review your Vault history to identify patterns of drift or progress.

You saw it.

The injustice. The lie. The pattern.
The thing that needed to be said.

And you said nothing.

Not because you didn't care.
Because speaking would cost you something.
Comfort. Reputation. Access. Peace.

---

Peace purchased by cowardice is not peace.
It's a ceasefire you're paying for with your integrity.

Every silence you keep to protect your comfort
is a small betrayal of the person you claim to be.

This week, name one truth you have avoided
because comfort benefited you.

Then decide:
Will you keep paying for peace with silence?
Or will you finally speak and trust what comes after?

Neutrality is often just fear with better manners.

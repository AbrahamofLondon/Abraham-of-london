You take pride in your work.

You deliver. You over-deliver.
You're known for quality.

But excellence has become more than a standard.
It's become a shield.

As long as your work is impressive,
no one asks how you're doing.
As long as your output is excellent,
no one sees the parts that aren't.

---

Excellence is a gift.
But when it replaces honesty,
it becomes armour.

And armour that never comes off
keeps everyone at a distance —
including the people who actually love you.

This week, let someone see something unfinished.

Not because it's bad.
Because connection doesn't happen at the level of performance.
It happens in the gaps.

You don't have to be impressive to be loved.
You just have to be real.

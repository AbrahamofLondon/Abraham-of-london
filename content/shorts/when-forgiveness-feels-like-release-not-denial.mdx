You've been told to forgive.

But no one told you that forgiveness
does not mean pretending it didn't happen.

It did happen.
It hurt.
It changed things.

Forgiveness is not about editing the past.
It's about refusing to be owned by it.

---

You can release the debt
without reopening the door.

You can forgive
without restoring trust.

You can let go of the bitterness
without pretending the wound wasn't real.

This week, try this:

Write what happened. Honestly.
Then write what you are choosing to release.

Not for them.
For you.

You can forgive and still be wise.
You can release and still protect your future.

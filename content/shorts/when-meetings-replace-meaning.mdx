Look at your calendar.

How many meetings this week?
How many could have been an email?
How many produced a decision?

Meetings have become the theatre of productivity.
They create the feeling of progress
without the reality of it.

You discuss instead of decide.
You align instead of act.
You brief instead of build.

---

Movement is not the same as momentum.

This week, cancel, shorten, or clarify one meeting.
Ask: does this need to happen, or does it just feel productive?

Protect your execution time like it's the only thing that matters.
Because it is.

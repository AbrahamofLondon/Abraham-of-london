There's a conversation you've been carrying.

Maybe for years.

The words you never said.
The apology you never made.
The truth you never spoke.

And it's been heavier than you realise.

---

Sometimes one honest conversation
can do more than ten years of avoidance.

Not because it fixes everything.
Because it breaks the silence that has been holding you captive.

This week, have or prepare one honest conversation.

Not a confrontation.
Just a truth that needs to be spoken.

You don't need to control the outcome.
You just need to stop carrying it alone.

Some healing begins with a single sentence.
And that sentence has been waiting long enough.

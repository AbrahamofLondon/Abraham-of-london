You've been trying to make them understand.

You've explained your decisions.
You've justified your path.
You've tried to help them see why you're doing what you're doing.

But they don't get it.

And maybe they never will.

---

Not everyone is meant to understand your journey.
Some people are only meant to witness it from a distance.
And some are not meant to be part of it at all.

Their lack of understanding
is not a sign you're wrong.
It's a sign the path is yours alone.

This week, stop overexplaining to the wrong audience.

You don't need everyone's approval to walk the path you've been given.
You just need the courage to keep walking.

The people who are meant to walk with you
will not need you to justify your steps.
They will recognise the path
because they are on it too.

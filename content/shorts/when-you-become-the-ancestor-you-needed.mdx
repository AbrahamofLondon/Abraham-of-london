Someone before you couldn't break the cycle.

They passed down the wound.
Not because they wanted to.
Because they didn't know how to stop it.

But you do.

You've done the work.
You've seen the pattern.
You know what ends with you.

---

Becoming the ancestor you needed
is one of the most powerful things you can do.

Not because it's easy.
Because it changes everything for those who come after you.

This week, choose one pattern that ends with you.

One thing you will not pass down.
One wound that stops here.
One cycle that closes in your generation.

You cannot change what came before you.
But you can change what comes after.
And that is a kind of immortality.

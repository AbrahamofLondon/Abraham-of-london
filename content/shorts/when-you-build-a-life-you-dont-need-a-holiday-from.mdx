You've been living for the weekend.

For the escape. For the break.
For the few days when you don't have to be
the person your week demands.

But what if your life didn't require escape?

What if your ordinary days
were sustainable enough that you didn't need
to recover from them?

---

A life that requires constant escape
is not a life. It's a sentence.

And you are allowed to redesign it.

This week, identify one weekday pressure point.
One thing that makes your ordinary days unsustainable.

Then change it.

Not your whole life.
Just one thing.

You don't have to wait for Friday to feel alive.
You can build a life you don't need to escape from.

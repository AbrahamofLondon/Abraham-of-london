You're in a hard season.

And you keep asking God:
"Is this a test or a consequence?"

Is this refining or reaping?
Is He building character or letting me feel what I've sown?

The answer matters because it changes what you do next.

If it's a test, you endure.
If it's a consequence, you repent.

---

Sometimes suffering is spiritual formation.
Sometimes it's harvest.

And the honest question is not always comfortable:
What obedience did I ignore?
What warning did I dismiss?
What boundary did I cross?

This week, don't just ask God to remove the difficulty.
Ask Him what it's revealing.

You may find that the trial and the lesson
are the same thing.

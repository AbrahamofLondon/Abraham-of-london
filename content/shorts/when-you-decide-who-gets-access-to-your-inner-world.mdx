You used to give everyone access.

Your story. Your struggles. Your inner world.
Available to anyone who asked.

But not everyone who asks for access
has earned the right to it.

Some people will use what you share against you.
Some will not know what to do with it.
Some will treat your vulnerability as entertainment.

---

Vulnerability is not a requirement for everyone.
It's a gift for those who have earned it.

And deciding who gets access to your inner world
is not secrecy. It's wisdom.

This week, share something real with someone who has earned it.

Not everyone. Just one person who has proven trustworthy.

Your inner world is precious.
And not everyone deserves a key.

You used to look for God in the spectacular.

The mountain-top experience.
The emotional worship service.
The moment of dramatic breakthrough.

And those moments matter.

But you're learning something new.

God is also in the ordinary.
The quiet morning. The mundane task.
The rhythm of daily faithfulness that no one sees.

---

If you only look for God in the extraordinary,
you'll miss Him in the life you're actually living.

This week, notice God in one ordinary moment.

The provision that arrived just in time.
The patience you didn't know you had.
The peace that came without explanation.

God is not only in the burning bush.
He is also in the daily bread.
And the daily bread is enough.

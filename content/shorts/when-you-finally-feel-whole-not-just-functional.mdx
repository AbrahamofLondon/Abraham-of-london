For a long time, you were functional.

You got up. You worked. You fulfilled your responsibilities.
You looked fine from the outside.

But inside, you were fragmented.
Parts of you were disconnected.
You were surviving, not living.

But something is changing.

You're starting to feel whole.
Not because everything is perfect.
Because the parts of you are coming back together.

---

Wholeness is not the absence of struggle.
It's the integration of all the parts of you —
the wounded, the strong, the afraid, the hopeful —
into one person who can face life as themselves.

This week, notice where integration is happening.

Where are the parts of you coming together?
Where do you feel more like yourself than you used to?

Wholeness is not a destination.
It's a returning.
And you are closer than you think.

You've been trained to suppress what you want.

To be realistic. To be reasonable.
To want only what is achievable.

But somewhere beneath the practicality,
there is a desire you've been afraid to name.

Not a fantasy. Not an escape.
A genuine want that has been waiting for permission.

---

Suppressed desire doesn't disappear.
It goes underground.
And it takes your aliveness with it.

This week, name one thing you actually want.
Without editing it. Without making it practical.
Just name it.

You don't have to act on it yet.
But you have to be honest about it.

You cannot move toward what you will not acknowledge.
And your desire is not a mistake.
It's a signal.

Your obedience is not just about you.

The steps you take — or don't take —
ripple beyond your own life.

Someone is watching.
Someone is waiting.
Someone's future is connected to your faithfulness.

Not because you're essential.
Because you're part of a story larger than yourself.

---

When you live like your obedience matters,
small decisions become significant.

The discipline you practise today
may be the foundation someone else builds on tomorrow.

This week, obey in one area that affects others.

Not for their approval.
For their good.

Your faithfulness is not just your business.
It's part of a chain.
And the people after you are counting on you to hold your link.

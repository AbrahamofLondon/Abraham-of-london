Someone is giving you attention.

They notice you. They pursue you.
They make you feel seen.

And it feels like love.

But attention and love are not the same thing.

Attention is exciting.
Love is steady.

Attention notices what's impressive.
Love notices what's heavy.

Attention wants to consume.
Love wants to protect.

---

This week, look at the people in your life.
Identify who gives you attention versus who actually cares for you.

One will leave when the novelty fades.
The other will stay when the novelty is gone.

Don't confuse the two.
Your heart is too valuable to waste on people
who only want what you offer, not who you are.

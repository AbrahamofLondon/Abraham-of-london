You're good at planning.

You've mapped it out. You've considered every angle.
You've waited until you were ready.

But ready never arrives.

Because readiness is not a destination.
It's a decision.

And while you've been planning,
the world has been moving.

---

Execution is not the enemy of quality.
It's the birthplace of it.

And the gap between planning and doing
is where most potential dies.

This week, ship one imperfect thing.

Not reckless. Not careless.
Just finished.

You can refine later.
But you cannot refine what you never release.

Done is better than perfect.
Because done actually exists in the world.

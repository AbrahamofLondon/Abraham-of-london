You're in a hurry.

You want answers now.
You want breakthrough now.
You want the waiting to be over.

But God is not in a hurry.

Not because He doesn't care.
Because He sees what you cannot.
Because the preparation matters as much as the arrival.
Because some things cannot be rushed without being ruined.

---

God's slowness is not neglect.
It's wisdom.

And the time you spend waiting
is not wasted. It's formative.

This week, release one timeline you've been gripping.

Not your hope. Just your deadline.

God is not late.
He is working at the speed of formation.
And formation cannot be accelerated without loss.

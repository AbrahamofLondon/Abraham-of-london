This is not about blame.

It's about honesty.

Some of the pain you're in right now
is not something that happened to you.
It's something you participated in creating.

The relationship you knew was unhealthy.
The financial decision you knew was reckless.
The boundary you knew you should have set.

---

Accountability is not self-hatred.
It's the adult recognition that your choices matter.

And the good news is:
if you chose this, you can choose differently.

This week, name one choice that led to your current pain.
Not with shame. With honesty.

Then ask: what choice can I make now
that begins to move me in a different direction?

You cannot change what you won't acknowledge.
But once you do, you can start again.

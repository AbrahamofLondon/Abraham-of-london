You've compartmentalised.

Sacred over here. Secular over there.
Worship on Sunday. Work on Monday.
Prayer in the morning. Life the rest of the day.

But the division is artificial.

Your whole life is a prayer.
Not just the words you speak in devotion.
But the choices you make, the way you work,
the patience you offer, the integrity you hold.

---

Every act of faithfulness is worship.
Every honest word is an offering.
Every moment of integrity is a prayer.

This week, offer one ordinary action as prayer.

Wash the dishes with presence.
Work with integrity.
Speak with kindness.

Not for show. For God.

Your life is saying something.
Make sure it's saying what you mean.

You've been living like there's unlimited time.

Like you can always start later.
Like the important thing can wait until you're ready.

But something has shifted.

You've become aware — maybe for the first time —
that this life is not a rehearsal.

You don't get to do it again.
This is it.

---

The awareness of mortality
is not meant to frighten you.
It's meant to focus you.

This week, make one irreversible adult move.

Send the application.
Have the conversation.
Make the commitment.
Start the thing you've been saving for "someday."

Someday is now.
And now is all you have.

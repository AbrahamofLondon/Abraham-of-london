You know the conversation.

The one that goes nowhere.
The complaint that never leads to action.
The venting that never becomes resolution.
The same topic, same frustration, same outcome.

And you're tired of carrying it.

---

Not every conversation deserves your energy.
Some are not dialogue.
They are emotional recycling.

And you are allowed to opt out.

This week, end one circular conversation early.

Not with cruelty.
With clarity.

"I can't keep having this conversation."
"I've said what I can say."
"I think we've reached the limit of what this discussion can offer."

You are not a receptacle for everyone's unprocessed frustration.
You are allowed to protect your peace.

You used to trust yourself.

You knew what you felt.
You knew what you needed.
You knew when something was wrong.

But somewhere along the way,
you learned to doubt your own instincts.

You started second-guessing.
You started asking everyone else what they thought.
You started ignoring the quiet voice
that had always been reliable.

---

Your instincts have not left you.
They've just been buried under noise.

And they are waiting for you to listen again.

This week, follow one gut feeling without over-analysing it.

Not recklessly. Just trustingly.

The voice you hear is not random.
It's the accumulated wisdom of everything you've lived.
And it deserves to be heard.

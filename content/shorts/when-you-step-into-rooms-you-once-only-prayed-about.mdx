You're here.

The room you prayed about.
The opportunity you asked for.
The door you hoped would open.

And now you're standing in it.

The temptation is to prove you belong.
To perform. To impress. To justify your presence.

But you don't need to prove anything.
You were brought here.
And the same God who opened the door
will sustain you inside it.

---

Arrival is not the end of faith.
It's a new kind of dependence.

This week, prepare instead of perform.
Focus on what you can offer, not how you appear.
Trust that you were led here for a reason.

You don't need to earn a place you were given.
You just need to steward it.

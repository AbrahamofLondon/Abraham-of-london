You don't owe anyone an explanation for your distance.

Not every withdrawal needs a statement.
Not every boundary needs a meeting.
Not every season of space needs a formal announcement.

---

Distance is not always rejection.
Sometimes it's wisdom.

And the people who respect you
will not need you to justify your need for space.

This week, maintain distance without issuing a public statement.

Let your absence speak for itself.
You don't need to explain, defend, or announce.

The right people will understand without an explanation.
And the wrong ones won't accept one anyway.

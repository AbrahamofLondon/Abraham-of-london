You share so much.

Your wins. Your insights. Your journey.
The parts of your life that look good online.

But there are parts you don't post.

The struggle that has no caption.
The pain that can't be summarised.
The area of your life that is still being formed
and is not ready for public consumption.

---

And those hidden parts —
the ones no one sees —
are exactly the parts God wants to meet you in.

Not the curated version.
The real one.

This week, pray about one thing nobody sees.

Not to perform. Not to share.
Just to bring it into the light between you and God.

The parts you hide are not a shame.
They are the ground of your deepest formation.
And they are safe with Him.

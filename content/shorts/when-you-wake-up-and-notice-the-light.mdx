You've been in a long night.

Not a literal one.
A season of darkness that felt like it would never end.

But this morning, something is different.

You woke up and noticed the light.

Not a dramatic sunrise.
Just a quiet shift in the atmosphere.
A sense that the night has passed,
even if the sun is not fully visible yet.

---

Hope does not always arrive with fanfare.
Sometimes it arrives as the ability
to notice that the darkness is not as thick as it was.

This week, notice one sign that morning has come.

Not the full breakthrough.
Just the evidence that the night is ending.

The fact that you felt something like hope.
The fact that you made it through.
The fact that you're still here.

The light is returning.
And you are still here to see it.

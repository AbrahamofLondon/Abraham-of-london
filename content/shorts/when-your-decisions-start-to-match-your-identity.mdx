Something is happening.

You're starting to make decisions
that match who you say you are.

Not perfectly. Not all the time.
But more than before.

You chose honesty over comfort.
You chose discipline over impulse.
You chose alignment over approval.

---

This is not a small thing.

Integrity is not a single heroic act.
It's the accumulation of small alignments
that eventually become a life.

This week, make one decision
from the person you are becoming.

Not the person you used to be.
Not the person others expect you to be.
The person you are becoming.

Each aligned decision is a brick.
And you're building something real.

You've been fighting for freedom.

And now you have it.

The thing that was holding you back is gone.
The constraint that defined your life has lifted.

But instead of relief, you feel fear.

Because freedom means choice.
And choice means responsibility.
And responsibility means you can no longer blame circumstances
for where you end up.

---

Freedom is not the absence of constraints.
It's the presence of agency.

And agency is terrifying
until you learn to use it.

This week, choose one direction instead of waiting for certainty.

You don't need the whole map.
You just need to take one step.

Certainty is not a prerequisite for movement.
Movement is how certainty is built.

You have something.

A gift. A talent. A capacity.
Something you were made to offer.

And it's sitting unused.

Not because you don't want to use it.
Because you're waiting.

Waiting for permission.
Waiting for the right opportunity.
Waiting to be discovered.

---

A gift unused is not a gift.
It's a weight.

And waiting to be discovered
is not humility. It's avoidance.

This week, use one gift privately.
Not for applause. Not for a platform.
Just use it.

Write the thing. Build the thing. Offer the thing.
Even if no one sees it.

Stewardship begins before the audience arrives.
And the person you become by using your gift
is more important than the recognition you hope it brings.

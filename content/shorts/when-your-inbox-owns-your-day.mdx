Your inbox is not a mission statement.
It's other people's priorities arriving uninvited.

And if you start your day there,
you've already surrendered direction
before you've had coffee.

---

Reactive living feels productive.
But it's just organised chaos.

You respond. You clear. You triage.
But at the end of the day,
did you move toward what matters?
Or did you just keep the noise at bay?

This week, try something different:

Before you open a single message,
spend one hour on what you decided matters.
Not what arrived. What you chose.

One protected deep-work block
before you let the world back in.

Direction is a decision.
Reaction is a default.
Choose which runs your day.

You've been working toward this.

The alignment between what you say
and what you do.

And it's starting to show.

Your calendar reflects your priorities.
Your speech matches your convictions.
Your money goes where your values are.
Your relationships are consistent with your standards.

Not perfectly. But recognisably.

---

Integrity is not a single act.
It's the accumulated result of thousands of small alignments.

And when your life finally looks like your values,
you don't have to explain yourself.
Your life already does the talking.

This week, protect one aligned habit.
One practice that keeps your life consistent with your values.

Guard it. Because it's not just a habit.
It's a brick in the house of integrity you're building.

And a life that matches your values
is the most persuasive thing you will ever offer.

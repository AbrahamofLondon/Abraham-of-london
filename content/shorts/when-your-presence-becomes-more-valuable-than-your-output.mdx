You've built your identity on what you produce.

The work you deliver. The problems you solve.
The value you add.

But you're starting to realise
that the people in your life
don't need you to fix everything.

They need you to be present.

---

Your output will be forgotten.
Your presence will be remembered.

And the greatest gift you can offer
is not another solution.
It's your full attention.

This week, offer presence without fixing anything.

Just listen. Just stay. Just be.

You don't need to solve it.
You just need to be there.

And sometimes being there
is more valuable than anything you could produce.

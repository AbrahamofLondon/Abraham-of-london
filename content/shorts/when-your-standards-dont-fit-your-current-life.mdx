You have high standards.

You know what you should be doing.
You know the person you want to be.

But your current life doesn't support it.

Your routines don't match your ambitions.
Your habits don't match your values.
Your daily choices don't match your stated priorities.

---

Aspiration without infrastructure is not vision.
It's self-deception.

This week, stop raising the standard.
Start building the routine that could support it.

Pick one standard that matters most.
Then ask: what habit would make that standard inevitable?

Build that one thing.
Not the whole system. Just one.

Standards without systems are just guilt waiting to happen.

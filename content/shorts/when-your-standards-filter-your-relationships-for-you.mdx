You used to chase connection.

You pursued people who weren't sure about you.
You tried to earn affection.
You lowered your standards to keep people around.

But you're learning something different:

Your standards are not a wall.
They're a filter.

And the right people
will not need you to lower them.

---

Standards are not arrogance.
They're clarity.

And clarity doesn't keep the right people away.
It keeps the wrong people from wasting your time.

This week, let the filter work without chasing.

If someone can't meet your standards,
let them go without guilt.

The people who are meant for you
will not require you to shrink.
And the ones who do
were never meant to stay.

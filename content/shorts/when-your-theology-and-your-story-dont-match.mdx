You know what you believe.

You have sound doctrine.
You can articulate the truth.
You've built your life on solid theological ground.

And yet.

There's a gap between what you know to be true
and what you feel to be real.

You believe God is good.
But your heart is still wounded.

You believe He is sovereign.
But your story feels chaotic.

---

The gap between theology and experience
is not a sign of weak faith.
It's a sign that you're human.

And God is not threatened by your inconsistency.

This week, bring the contradiction to God.
Not to performance. Not to a small group.
Just to Him.

Say: "I believe this. But I feel this. Help me."

Honest prayer is more powerful than polished theology.
And God meets you in the gap.

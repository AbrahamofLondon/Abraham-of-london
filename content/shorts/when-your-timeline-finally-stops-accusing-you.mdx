You've been measuring yourself against a timeline.

By now you should have arrived.
By now you should have achieved.
By now you should be further along.

And the gap between where you are
and where you thought you'd be
has been a source of quiet shame.

---

But what if the timeline was wrong?
What if the pace you're moving at
is exactly the pace required for what you're becoming?

You're not behind.
You're on a different path.

This week, release the timeline that has been accusing you.
Not your goals. Just the arbitrary deadline.

Some things take longer because they're deeper.
And depth cannot be rushed.

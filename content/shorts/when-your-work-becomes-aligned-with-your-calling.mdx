You've felt the misalignment.

The work that drains instead of fuels.
The tasks that feel empty.
The career that pays the bills but costs your soul.

But something is changing.

You're starting to feel alignment.
The work you're doing is starting to match
who you were made to be.

---

Alignment doesn't mean the work is easy.
It means the work is meaningful.

And when your work is aligned with your calling,
even the hard days have purpose.

This week, identify one alignment signal.
One sign that your work and your calling are coming together.

It might be small.
A moment of flow. A task that felt like life.
A contribution that felt significant.

Notice it.
And let it encourage you.
You're moving in the right direction.

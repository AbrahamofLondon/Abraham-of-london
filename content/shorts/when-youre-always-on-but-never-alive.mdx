You're always ready.

Ready to perform. Ready to respond.
Ready to be the person everyone expects you to be.

But when was the last time you were just alive?
Not performing. Not optimising. Not curating.
Just present.

---

Performance mode is exhausting
because it never turns off.

Even when you're alone, you're still performing —
for the future audience in your head.

This week, create one hour that is not for anyone else.

No phone. No content. No production.
Just you, being alive without trying to look alive.

You are not a brand.
You are a person.
And persons need space that is not for consumption.

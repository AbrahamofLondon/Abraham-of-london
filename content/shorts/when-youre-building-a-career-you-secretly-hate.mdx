You're successful.

On paper, everything looks right.
The title. The income. The trajectory.

But you dread Monday.
You feel empty after the wins.
You wonder if this is all there is.

---

You followed respectability into quiet misery.

The career you built was never wrong.
It just wasn't yours.

This week, do an honest audit:

Write two columns.
What this career gives you.
What it costs you.

Be honest about both.
Then ask yourself:
Is the cost worth what you're receiving?

You are allowed to want something different.
Even if it looks impressive from the outside.

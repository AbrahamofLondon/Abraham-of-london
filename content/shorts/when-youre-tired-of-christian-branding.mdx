You're tired of the performance.

The curated faith. The aesthetic spirituality.
The posts designed to look holy
while the private life stays unexamined.

You're tired of people using God as a brand
and calling it ministry.

And maybe — if you're honest —
you're tired of your own participation in it.

---

Faith was never meant to be a platform.
It was meant to be a life.

And the most powerful acts of obedience
are usually the ones no one sees.

This week, choose hidden obedience over visible signalling.

Pray without posting it.
Give without announcing it.
Serve without branding it.

God sees what is done in secret.
And that is enough.

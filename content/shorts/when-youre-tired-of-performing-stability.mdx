You're the one everyone relies on.

The stable one. The calm one.
The one who never falls apart.

But inside, you're exhausted.

Not from the work.
From the performance of being fine
when you're not fine.

---

Strength is not the absence of struggle.
It's the ability to keep going while struggling.

But even strength needs honesty.

You can be strong and still admit you're tired.
You can be reliable and still need help.
You can be stable and still have cracks.

This week, do one brave thing:

Tell one safe person the truth about how you're doing.
Not the edited version. The real one.

You don't have to perform stability to be steady.
Real steadiness includes the admission that some days are hard.

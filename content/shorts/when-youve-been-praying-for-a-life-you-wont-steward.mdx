You keep asking God for more.

More influence. More responsibility.
More of the thing you think will satisfy.

But look at what you already have.

Are you stewarding it?
Or are you neglecting what's in your hand
while reaching for what's not yet yours?

---

Prayer is not a way to bypass stewardship.
It's a way to align with it.

And God is unlikely to entrust you with more
when you're mismanaging what you already have.

This week, stop asking for more.
Start stewarding what's in front of you.

The relationships you have.
The resources you've been given.
The responsibilities already on your plate.

Faithfulness in small things
is the only path to greater things.

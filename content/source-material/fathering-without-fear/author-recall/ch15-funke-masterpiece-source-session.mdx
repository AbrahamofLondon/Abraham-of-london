## Revised Chapter Questions

**Primary:** What happens when the woman who organised the family's ordinary life disappears just before the house breaks open?

**Secondary:** What does it mean for the first daughter of the first son to carry family order with pride and joy, then vanish before the next collapse can be named?

The chapter must hold both. The first governs the death sequence. The second governs who Funke was alive.

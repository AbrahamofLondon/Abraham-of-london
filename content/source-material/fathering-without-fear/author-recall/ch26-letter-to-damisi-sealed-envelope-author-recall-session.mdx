# Source facts (verbatim from author, nothing added)

- Context: Abraham was going into a major surgery.
- Location: Chelsea and Westminster Hospital.
- Underlying question that prompted the letter: whether Damisi would ever know the truth of who his father really was, and what actually happened, if Abraham did not survive or was not able to tell him directly.
- Abraham wrote a letter to Damisi.
- The letter contains: a detailed account of "his side of the story," referencing available evidence in public records and private conversations.
- The letter also contains: passwords to all social media accounts and devices.
- Specific instruction attached to the letter: the envelope is private, confidential, and legally privileged.
- Specific instruction on timing: the envelope is to be given to Damisi only on his 18th birthday — not before.

# Chapter 19 Damisi Author Recall Session

## Purpose

Gather source material for a future Ch.19 expansion while preserving the chapter's central rule:

Damisi must remain a boy.

Ch.17 is the contact room. Ch.18 is seven years of duration, route, and staying. Ch.19 is Damisi as a child, irreducible to the structure around him.

This file is source material only. It does not revise Ch.19 prose. It collects confirmed fragments already present in the manuscript and prior recall files, classifies them for public use, and identifies the author recall still needed before any expansion draft.

Core sentence to protect unless the prose earns a better version:

```md
A child should be allowed to like tigers without becoming a symbol.
```

## Child-Protection Locks

- Damisi must not become proof.
- Damisi must not become evidence.
- Damisi must not become argument.
- Damisi must not become symbol.
- Damisi must not become diagnosis.
- Damisi must not become a moral burden.
- Damisi must not become a spiritual sign.
- Damisi must not become a legal exhibit.
- Damisi must not become an emotional weapon.
- Do not use Damisi's words to prove adult claims.
- Do not infer psychological condition, cause, motive, or pressure.
- Do not include sealed-order material, report disputes, professional assessments, allegation detail, procedural argument, or adversarial parent framing.
- Do not make his sadness carry the chapter. Let ordinary boyhood carry it.
- The memoir may protect a detail by leaving it out.

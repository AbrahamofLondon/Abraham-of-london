# Chapter 20 Love Author Recall Session

## Purpose

Gather source material for expanding Ch.20 without drafting Ch.20 prose.

Ch.20 follows the fatherhood sequence:

- Ch.17: the contact room.
- Ch.18: seven years.
- Ch.19: Damisi as a boy.
- Ch.20: Abraham rereading love, fear, courage, discernment, and the misuse of conviction.

The chapter must become a room, a Bible verse, a man rereading himself, a sentence that was true but misused, and a corrected understanding of courage. It must not become a sermon, self-help chapter, theological essay, marriage post-mortem, spiritual proof-text, revenge against the past self, or abstract commentary after the strongest child chapters.

Non-negotiable effect:

```md
The verse did not change.
The man reading it did.
```

Note: `project_fwf_canon.md` remains absent from the workspace. This session relies on the prose skeleton, Ch.20 draft, Ch.12 recall session, and Ch.17-Ch.19 recall sessions.

## Theological / Privacy Locks

- Do not preach the conclusion. Let Abraham discover the correction.
- Do not make 1 John 4:18 into a proof-text for a new argument.
- Do not imply the verse was false. The standard is: the verse remains true; Abraham's former use of it becomes unavailable.
- Do not mock providence, prayer, provision, faith, obedience, or spiritual interpretation.
- Do not deny that provision may be mercy. The correction is that provision is not always permission.
- Do not use Ch.20 to retry the marriage, name the bride, diagnose anyone, or attribute motives.
- Do not make Damisi evidence of anything. Fatherhood teaches restraint here; Damisi does not become proof.
- Do not make Abraham's younger self contemptible. The chapter's authority depends on self-accounting without self-revenge.
- Do not use legal proceedings, sealed records, report disputes, or court-adjacent specifics.
- Do not quote or reproduce private journals, notes, messages, or Bible-margin annotations unless the author confirms they are public-safe.
- Keep the supernatural register plain: no fire, voice, trance, or crisis revelation in the Ch.20 writing scene.
- The writing room is the carrying scene. It needs one physical detail before expansion: desk, chair, laptop, notebook, light, time of day, sound, window, table, or what the page looked like.

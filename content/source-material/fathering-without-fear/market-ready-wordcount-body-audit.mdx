# Market-Ready Word Count & Body Audit

**Standard chapter length ranges:**

| Type | Target |
|---|---|
| Major chapter | 1,200–2,000 words |
| Standard chapter | 900–1,500 words |
| Short hinge chapter | 700–1,000 words |
| Deliberate interlude | 500–800 words |
| Under 500 words | Not acceptable unless formally designated as fragment/interlude |

## Executive Summary

Institutional governance within this context is not merely administrative; it is a sovereign necessity. This brief outlines the protocols for decision-making, asset protection, and the maintenance of link-integrity across the digital and physical vault.

## I. [Governance](/lexicon/governance) Framework

The architecture is built upon three primary pillars:
1. **Sovereign Accountability:** Ensuring all intelligence briefs are vetted and verified.
2. **Systemic Resilience:** Maintaining the "Vault-Only" mode to prevent external dependencies.
3. **Strategic Oversight:** Aligning document production with the long-term portfolio objectives (Current Portfolio: 75 Briefs).

### The Three Fractures of Economic Decay

#### 1. The Permission Trap
Most wealth today exists as a digital entry in a system that can be paused or seized by a third party. This is a total violation of **[Sovereignty](/lexicon/sovereignty)**. If your capital can be "turned off" due to your convictions, you do not own that capital; you are merely renting it from the state.

#### 2. The Debt Anchor
Debt is the primary mechanism of **[Responsibility](/lexicon/responsibility)**-avoidance. It allows for the illusion of growth while sacrificing future [Strategic Autonomy](/lexicon/strategic-autonomy). In the Canon, a fortress is built with stones you already own. A leveraged life is a life under the authority of the lender.

#### 3. Asset Dissipation
The lack of a **[Covenant](/lexicon/covenant)**-based family agreement leads to the "Shirtsleeves to Shirtsleeves" decay. Capital is treated as a personal reward for the individual rather than an institutional tool for the Great Restoration.

### The Strategic Pivot: The Fortress Protocol

To move your economy from **Decay** to **Rise**, three "Plumb Line" corrections are mandatory:

1. **The Seizure-Resistance Audit**: A minimum of 30% of the total [Ledger](/lexicon/legacy) must be converted into bearer assets (physical gold, decentralized protocols, or private land) that cannot be liquidated by a centralized "kill switch."
2. **The Debt-Purge Mandate**: Systematic elimination of all consumer and high-interest debt to recover the "Power of the No."
3. **The Mission-Aligned Ledger**: Active divestment from institutions and funds that fund the dissolution of the [Family](/lexicon/family) and the office of the [Father](/lexicon/father).

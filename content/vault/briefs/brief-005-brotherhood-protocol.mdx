### The Strategic Pivot: The Phalanx Protocol

To transition your alliances from **Decay** to **Rise**, you must implement the **Three Gates of Entry**:

2. **The Shared Burden**: Alignment is proven through labor, not talk. You do not know a man until you have carried a [Responsibility](/lexicon/responsibility) with him that neither of you could carry alone.

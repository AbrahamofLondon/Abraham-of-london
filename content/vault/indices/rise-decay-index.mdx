# Volume IV: The Rise-Decay Index

<Verse>
"The Rise-Decay Index is the triage tool for the Great Restoration. It measures the 'Institutional Delta' between Ascension (Order) and Entropy (Decay)."
</Verse>

In the Canon, we do not guess at the health of a build; we measure its resistance to chaos. This index provides a high-tensile framework to audit any entity—whether a family office, a business, or a potential covenantal ally—against the **14 Standardized Metrics of Power**.

## How to Use the Index

### 1. Select a Subject
Define the boundary of the audit (e.g., Your [Family](/vault/lexicon/family) Office, your current Business, or a potential [Brotherhood](/lexicon/brotherhood) candidate).

### 2. Audit the Terms
Rate the subject's current operational state on each of the 14 terms. A metric is only marked as **"Rise"** if the canonical standard is consistently met under pressure.

### 3. Calculate the Delta
Total the number of "Rise" indicators to determine the **Institutional Grade**:

| Score | Classification | Interpretation |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| **10–14** | Restoration Anchor | The institution is structurally sound and built for the 100-year horizon. It possesses [Strategic Autonomy](/lexicon/strategic-autonomy). |
| **5–9** | Transitionary State | The institution possesses Order but carries "Systemic Residue" (dependency/noise) that must be purged to avoid fracture. |
| **0–4** | Active Decay | The entity is a liability. It is currently being consumed by entropy and will be absorbed by the coming chaos. |

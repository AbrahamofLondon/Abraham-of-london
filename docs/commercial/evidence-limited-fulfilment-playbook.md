# Evidence-Limited Fulfilment Playbook

This playbook governs first-revenue fulfilment for evidence-limited offers while product authority remains non-restored.

## Scope

Current manually fulfilled offers:

- `fast_diagnostic_decision_support`
- `enterprise_assessment_advisory_review`

These offers are sellable decision-support or advisory-review services. They do not grant product authority, external proof, or independent verification.

## Fulfilment Sequence

1. Order intent received
   - Source may be the offer route, contact form, email, or advisory call request.
   - Record offer code, buyer name, organisation, email, request type, and requested timeline.

2. Buyer intake submitted
   - Collect decision description, stakeholders, deadline, evidence available, constraints, desired outcome, prior attempts, options under consideration, and consequence of delay.
   - For enterprise review, also collect organisation context, affected functions, decision owner, sponsor, and any documents the buyer wants considered.

3. Evidence boundary accepted
   - Buyer must acknowledge that the output is evidence-limited decision-support material.
   - Buyer must acknowledge that user-supplied material is not independently verified by the offer.
   - Buyer must acknowledge that the output does not grant validated product authority.

4. Manual invoice or payment link issued
   - Use assisted billing until self-serve checkout is explicitly approved for these offer codes.
   - Do not create or imply product authority from payment.

5. Diagnostic or advisory output generated
   - Prepare the output from the submitted intake and any supplied evidence.
   - Mark claims as user-supplied, inferred, unsupported, or requiring evidence where relevant.
   - Include limitations and next evidence action.

6. Human review completed where purchased
   - Confirm the output stays within the purchased scope.
   - Remove unsupported authority language.
   - Confirm no claim of restored authority, external proof, or outcome assurance appears.

7. Delivery email sent
   - Attach or link the output.
   - Repeat the evidence boundary.
   - Include the recommended next evidence action.

8. Case archived
   - Archive intake, boundary acceptance, payment reference, output version, delivery timestamp, and operator notes.
   - Do not use the case as validation evidence unless a separate validation protocol is run.

## Required Boundary Text

This is evidence-limited decision-support material. It is designed to structure judgement, expose risk, and support review. It is not independently verified authority evidence and does not grant validated product authority.

## Operator Checks Before Delivery

- The offer code is recorded.
- The buyer accepted the evidence boundary.
- The output states what it can and cannot support.
- User-supplied claims are not presented as independently verified.
- The output includes limitations and next evidence action.
- Payment or invoice reference is recorded.
- No restored-authority language appears.

## Launch Boundary

These offers may be sold through manual fulfilment. They must not be represented as authority restored, externally proven, certified, or guaranteed. They are commercial activation surfaces, not product validation decisions.

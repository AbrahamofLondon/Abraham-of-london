## Release Workflow

On each release date, the action is the same:

1. **Set `draft: false`** on the file(s) releasing that day
2. **Commit and push** to `main`
3. Deployment happens automatically

The `seriesVisibility: "scheduled"` field stays in place — it only affects series card visibility, not individual part readability. Once a part has `draft: false` and its date is past, the classifier automatically promotes it to `PUBLIC_READABLE_NOW`.

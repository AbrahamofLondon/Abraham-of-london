# Founding-Customer Programme

Scope: 3 to 5 organisations, 8 weeks, one live decision each.

Eligibility: named decision owner, material consequence, willingness to provide evidence at intake and checkpoint, authority to act, and consent for anonymised operational learning.

Excluded: generic coaching, unlimited bespoke consulting, speculative strategy without an owner, and regulated/confidential work without separate assurance review.

Product corridor: Decision Signal -> Corridor -> Operator Pilot -> scoped controlled continuation where accepted -> checkpoint review -> Decision Centre continuity.

Checkpoints: intake, qualification, scope, required move, 30-day outcome review, closing evidence review.

Support boundary: one decision, one owner, one checkpoint cadence. Out-of-scope changes require amendment rather than free consulting drift.

Pricing: founding controlled pilot price to be quoted manually after qualification; no public checkout until resolver permits it.

Privacy and consent: no durable personal history from anonymous Signal; explicit identity and consent are required before case binding. Confidential intake text is excluded from analytics.

Publication options: private only, anonymised pattern, named case study. Named publication requires separate written approval.

Success criteria: completed decision history, at least one admissible corridor change, operator qualification evidence, checkpoint evidence, and honest outcome classification.

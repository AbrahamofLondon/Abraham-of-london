# Assessment Role Clarity Map

> Date: 2026-05-08
> Principle: Every surface has one job. No surface copies another.

---

## Fast Diagnostic
**Role:** First contradiction capture. Three questions. One commitment gate.
**Must capture:** The decision, the authority, the cost of delay.
**Must not become:** Full strategy intake, personality quiz, or coaching warm-up.
**Emotional register:** Sharp and immediate. "You are stopped at the door with one brutal truth."
**Current status:** STRONG — all 4 questions earn their place.

## Purpose Alignment
**Role:** Personal operating alignment and mandate clarity.
**Must capture:** What is avoided, what competes, what deteriorates. Identity, decision integrity, environment, behaviour, emotional order, legacy orientation.
**Must not become:** Therapeutic self-help, values inventory, or generic wellness assessment.
**Emotional register:** Personal and reflective but evidence-bound. "You are made to look at your own operating pattern."
**Current status:** STRONG after cleanup — 8 questions rewritten, zero generic statements remain.

## Constitutional Diagnostic
**Role:** Governance posture and decision authority diagnosis.
**Must capture:** Authority clarity, trust under constraint, coherence, correction failure patterns, external urgency, intervention readiness.
**Must not become:** Corporate culture survey, leadership personality assessment, or maturity model.
**Emotional register:** Institutional and structural. "An examination of authority and structure."
**Current status:** STRONG — q5/q9 fixed, evidence bridge created. Bridge enrichment needed for upstream context.

## Team Assessment
**Role:** Leader perception versus operating reality.
**Must capture:** Divergence between leader belief and team experience. Direction, execution, trust, authority, consequence awareness, disagreement quality.
**Must not become:** Employee engagement survey, satisfaction poll, or 360-degree feedback form.
**Emotional register:** Exposure. "Do I see my team accurately?"
**Current status:** Respondent STRONG (8 domains). Leader ADEQUATE (1 weak statement, no consequence pricing).

## Enterprise Assessment
**Role:** Institutional strain and board-level consequence reading.
**Must capture:** Leadership coherence, governance reliability, execution variance, institutional risk posture, political resistance, cost of delay.
**Must not become:** Maturity model checklist, compliance audit, or HR culture survey.
**Emotional register:** Gravity. "This institution may have structural problems."
**Current status:** Respondent STRONG (rebuilt). Leader boolean ADEQUATE (4 weak statements). Likert NOT TOUCHED.

## Executive Reporting
**Role:** Priced consequence and board-ready synthesis.
**Must capture:** The exact decision, the cost of delay, the real constraint, what has already failed, verification criteria.
**Must not become:** Generic paid report order form or consulting brief submission.
**Emotional register:** Precision. "This report will be exactly as good as your input."
**Current status:** STRONG — verification anchor added. Sequence ordering still identity-first (should be decision-first).

## Strategy Room
**Role:** Governed execution and intervention discipline.
**Must capture:** Problem structure, authority, consequence dimensions, readiness, blockers, capacity, commitment.
**Must not become:** Consultancy booking form, project intake, or workshop registration.
**Emotional register:** Weight. "This is the most serious thing you have done in this product."
**Current status:** Intake Stage 0-1 STRONG. Stage 2 sliders WEAK (evasion risk). Execution controls ADEQUATE (unexplained status transitions).

## Return Brief
**Role:** Memory, verification, and trajectory confrontation.
**Must capture:** Whether committed actions were executed. What changed, what did not, what was avoided, what must now happen.
**Must not become:** Recap page, summary dashboard, or progress report.
**Emotional register:** Confrontation. "The system remembers what you said you would do."
**Current status:** Prompts STRONG. Evidence consumption PARTIAL — does not use avoidedDecision or costOfDelay from upstream.

## Outcome Verification
**Role:** Evidence-based intervention effectiveness assessment.
**Must capture:** Whether the condition resolved, improved, stabilised, or worsened. Contradiction persistence. Root cause status.
**Must not become:** Satisfaction survey or progress report.
**Emotional register:** Truth. "Did it work?"
**Current status:** Display STRONG. No user-defined success criteria input consumed yet.

## Oversight Brief / Retainer
**Role:** Retainer-grade continuity and cancellation-loss intelligence.
**Must capture:** Recurrence, cost accumulation, option decay, irreversibility, counsel/board escalation triggers.
**Must not become:** Dashboard report, status update, or retainer justification letter.
**Emotional register:** Institutional awareness. "The institution stayed awake while the client drifted."
**Current status:** Display-only. NO dedicated intake question set. Retainer qualification is computed, not experiential.

## Counsel Workflow
**Role:** Governed human escalation with structured evidence.
**Must capture:** Why system governance is insufficient, what counsel must resolve, what evidence counsel adds, what risk exists if counsel's recommendation is ignored.
**Must not become:** Advice request form, email chain, or task assignment.
**Emotional register:** Gravity. "This requires human judgement the system cannot provide."
**Current status:** WEAK — auto-generated CRUD form with no customization, no guidance, no evidence structure.

## Control Room / Organisation Setup
**Role:** Organisation-level command memory and governance deployment.
**Must capture:** Organisation identity, mandate, sector, operating profile. Campaign scope, participant selection, cadence.
**Must not become:** Generic admin dashboard, CRM, or project management tool.
**Emotional register:** Institutional registration. "This organisation is entering governed oversight."
**Current status:** ADEQUATE — org setup has good description validation, campaign creation has bespoke title examples. Sector field and cadence field lack guidance.

## Access / Redemption
**Role:** Trust establishment and entitlement delivery.
**Must capture:** Key validity, entitlement scope, authentication state.
**Must not become:** E-commerce checkout or generic invite flow.
**Emotional register:** Institutional access. "You have been granted access to governed infrastructure."
**Current status:** STRONG — bespoke copy, specific error messages, format guidance.

# Bespoke Surface Enforcement Checklist

**Date:** 2026-05-07
**Rule:** No primary entry point may remain GENERIC RISK before broad public launch.

---

## Scoring criteria

A surface is scored based on:

1. Shows user-specific decision/context
2. Shows user-specific contradiction
3. Shows evidence basis
4. Shows missing evidence where applicable
5. Shows specific next action
6. Shows continuity where available
7. Avoids generic boilerplate

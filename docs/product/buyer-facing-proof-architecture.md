# Buyer-Facing Proof Architecture

**Date:** 2026-05-08
**Authority:** Decision Infrastructure by Abraham of London

---

## The Proof Journey

Proof has a journey, not a pile of badges.

### 1. Homepage — Process Proof
What the system does. Not what it has achieved.
No outcome claims unless approved aggregate proof exists (N>=15, sealed).
Show: evidence tested, contradiction found, directive issued, action required, verification checkpoints, refusal logic.

### 2. Fast Diagnostic — Personal Proof
"The system saw your contradiction."
User-specific findings. Not generalised claims. The proof is that the system responded to their specific input with governed analysis.

### 3. Executive Reporting — Case Proof
"This case qualifies or does not qualify for board-level treatment."
Evidence-gated admission. The proof is the admission decision itself — earned or refused.

### 4. Boardroom Mode — Decision Proof
"This is why the matter deserves board attention."
Structured dossier with evidence basis, consequence exposure, and escalation rationale.

### 5. Strategy Room — Behaviour Proof
"You committed, acted, blocked, or avoided."
Execution tracking. The proof is the recorded behaviour against stated commitments.

### 6. Return Brief — Follow-Through Proof
"This is what changed since the last session."
Delta metrics, trajectory, commitment verification checkpoints. The proof is visible change (or visible inaction).

### 7. Oversight Brief — Continuity Proof
"This is what the system preserved and what would have been missed."
Cycle-to-cycle comparison, value-at-risk, pattern recurrence. The proof is accumulated institutional memory.

### 8. Evidence Standards — Institutional Proof
"This is how evidence becomes publishable."
Disclosure of standards, methodology, and publication gates. The proof is the existence of the standards themselves.
Route: `/evidence/standards`

### 9. Metrics — Aggregate Proof
Only when sample size is sufficient (N>=15).
If insufficient: show standards and state that metrics are not yet issued.
If sufficient: show total approved outcomes, sample size, verification distribution, seal distribution.

### 10. Case Studies — Narrative Proof
Only SILVER+ and reviewed. Published through governed pipeline.
If fewer than 3 SILVER+ cases exist: do not create a public case studies page.

---

## What This Architecture Prevents

- Proof without journey (badges without context)
- Claims without evidence class
- Aggregate metrics without sample size
- Case studies without seal
- Public proof without human review
- Engine exposure through proof language
- Vanity metrics without methodology

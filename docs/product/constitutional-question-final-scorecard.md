# Constitutional Question Final Scorecard

> Date: 2026-05-08
> Standard: Every question must score at least 8/10 across 10 dimensions.

---

## Dimensions

1. **Evidence yield** -- does the answer produce usable diagnostic signal?
2. **Authority relevance** -- does it test who can decide or what blocks decision authority?
3. **Pressure relevance** -- does it test urgency, stakes, or environmental force?
4. **Contradiction detection** -- can it expose something that cannot be true at the same time?
5. **Governance usefulness** -- does it feed posture, readiness, or route classification?
6. **Emotional seriousness** -- does it feel like it matters?
7. **Clarity** -- can the user place themselves on a 0-10 scale without confusion?
8. **Scoring fit** -- does the domain assignment and polarity match the intent?
9. **Result usefulness** -- does it improve downstream reporting, routing, or intervention?
10. **Buyer confidence** -- does it make the product feel serious and institutional?

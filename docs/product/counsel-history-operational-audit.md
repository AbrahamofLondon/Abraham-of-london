# Counsel History Operational Audit

## Verdict

Counsel is now treated as governed escalation evidence, not informal advisory access.

## Questions answered

- Was counsel required?
- Why was counsel required?
- Was counsel review completed?
- Did counsel produce evidence?
- Was the outcome accepted, rejected, or still pending?

## Current status model used in history view

- `NOT_REQUIRED`
- `RECOMMENDED`
- `REQUIRED`
- `IN_REVIEW`
- `COMPLETED`
- `ESCALATED`
- `INEFFECTIVE`
- `OUTCOME_PENDING`

## Remaining gap

- outcome impact is still a conservative evidence-based summary, not a dedicated post-counsel outcome model

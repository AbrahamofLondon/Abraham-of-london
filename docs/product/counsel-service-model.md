# Counsel Service Model

**Date:** 2026-05-08
**Authority:** Decision Infrastructure by Abraham of London

---

## Principle

Counsel is governed escalation, not consulting theatre.

Counsel exists when the system has surfaced a case that exceeds its safe automated boundary.

---

## Tiers

### Counsel Review

- written review of the case
- examines dossier, contradiction graph, and decision log
- produces structured evidence node(s)

### Counsel Intervention

- counsel participates in Strategy Room or execution review
- commitments are explicitly tracked
- outcome movement is later verified

### Counsel Oversight

- retainer-only
- monitors multiple cases
- receives escalation triggers
- contributes to monthly oversight brief

---

## Hard rules

- counsel outputs must become evidence
- counsel recommendations must be logged
- counsel cannot bypass system evidence gates
- counsel involvement must be visible in decision history
- counsel is not an informal side channel around governance

# Intelligence Trust Language Register

Date: 2026-05-09

## Decision Velocity

Approved:

- "Measured from recorded checkpoints."
- "Not a benchmark."
- "A checkpoint has been scheduled. Velocity starts once an outcome is recorded."
- "Your average time from diagnosis to recorded response is X."
- "Compared with your previous recorded cycle."

Forbidden:

- "You are improving" without recorded comparative history
- "Benchmark" unless external governed comparison is genuinely active
- "Verified" unless a verification record exists

## What Changed

Approved:

- "Compared with your previous recorded state."
- "No prior comparable record yet."
- "This will become useful after another completed checkpoint, diagnostic, or outcome verification."

Forbidden:

- any change claim without two dated comparable records
- faux before/after language with one record
- undefined comparison windows

## Cross-Assessment Intelligence

Approved:

- "This pattern is inferred from multiple completed assessments."
- "The record suggests"
- "This may indicate"
- "The case has progressed into Strategy Room execution"

Forbidden:

- "The graph proves"
- "The kernel detected"
- "The system knows"
- "proved"

## Contradiction Preview

Approved:

- "The system detected tension between recorded signals."
- "Active contradiction"
- "First seen"
- "Last seen"
- "Next action"

Forbidden:

- hard-coded certainty language
- "verified" without outcome verification or operator review
- graph / node / edge / mechanic language

## Irreversibility

Approved:

- "This is an estimate based on available evidence."
- "Options may be narrowing."
- "This case is becoming harder to reverse."
- "Based on recorded signals."
- "Not independently verified."

Forbidden:

- "This is irreversible"
- "Options are closed" unless the record proves closure
- factor weights or internal formula language

## Benchmarks

Approved:

- "External benchmark unavailable. This is based only on your record."
- "Comparative positioning against an anonymised comparison set" only when claim governance permits it

Forbidden:

- "AI baseline"
- "market baseline"
- unsupported peer/cohort/percentile claims
- undeclared benchmark comparisons

## Verification

Approved:

- `reported`
- `measured`
- `inferred`
- `estimated`
- `reviewed`
- `verified` only when an actual verification record exists

Forbidden:

- calling self-report verified
- calling thin inference verified
- "quality check passed" as outcome proof

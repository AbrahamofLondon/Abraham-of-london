# Product Ladder Message Map

**Date:** 2026-05-09
**Purpose:** One-page reference for each product surface — what it is, what it asks, what it produces, what it remembers, what it unlocks, and what it must never claim.

---

## Stage 01 — Fast Diagnostic

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A 3-question decision exposure reader. The entry point to the system. |
| **What it asks** | What decision is unresolved? Who owns it? What gets worse if it stays unresolved? |
| **What it produces** | A structural finding, a required move, a cost-of-inaction estimate, and a 48-hour checkpoint. |
| **What it remembers** | The decision, the stated consequence, the commitment response, and the checkpoint outcome. |
| **What it unlocks** | Purpose Alignment Assessment, Constitutional Diagnostic, Decision Centre entry, Return Brief eligibility. |
| **What it must never claim** | "Pattern" from 3 inputs. "Verified" diagnosis. Financial precision. Independent validation. |
| **Evidence posture** | User-reported inputs. System-inferred finding. Estimated cost. Single-source evidence. |

---

## Stage 02 — Purpose Alignment Assessment

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A dual-axis personal decision alignment assessment using resonance and certainty scoring. |
| **What it asks** | What decision are you avoiding? What competing priority is pulling against it? What gets worse? Then 12 structural signals rated on two axes. |
| **What it produces** | An alignment profile, contradiction map, structural recommendations, and a governed checkpoint. |
| **What it remembers** | All dual-axis scores, contradictions, the alignment state, and checkpoint responses. |
| **What it unlocks** | Executive Reporting eligibility. Strategy Room consideration. Deeper diagnostic instruments. |
| **What it must never claim** | "Verdict." "Trust" without evidence. Psychometric validity. Personality classification. |
| **Evidence posture** | User-reported inputs. System-inferred alignment state. Dual-axis divergence is system-computed. |

---

## Stage 03 — Constitutional Diagnostic

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A multi-question structural assessment of organisational decision-making capability. |
| **What it asks** | 12 structural propositions rated on resonance and certainty. Evidence capture for escalation. |
| **What it produces** | A constitutional micro-report with route, posture, readiness, findings, risks, and downstream context. |
| **What it remembers** | All scores, the route decision, the evidence capture responses, and inherited team-level hypotheses. |
| **What it unlocks** | Executive Reporting. Strategy Room (when evidence warrants). Team/Enterprise Assessment paths. |
| **What it must never claim** | That the report is a full organisational diagnosis. "Proven" structural findings. Psychometric validity. |
| **Evidence posture** | User-reported via dual-axis. System-computed route and posture. System-inferred findings. |

---

## Stage 04 — Team Assessment

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A leader-estimated team perception gap analysis across Direction, Execution, Trust, and Authority. |
| **What it asks** | 12 domain-specific propositions. Leader rates their own view and estimates the team's view. |
| **What it produces** | A gap reading with pattern classification, severity band, and routing to Enterprise Assessment or Strategy Room. |
| **What it remembers** | All gap scores, the pattern reading, evidence capture responses, and checkpoint commitment. |
| **What it unlocks** | Enterprise Assessment. Strategy Room (when warranted). |
| **What it must never claim** | That it measured the team directly. "Team view" — it measured the leader's estimate of the team view. |
| **Evidence posture** | Leader-reported. Reality scores are leader-estimated, not team-reported. Gap is system-computed. |

---

## Stage 05 — Enterprise Assessment

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | An institutional-level diagnostic across Leadership Coherence, Governance Reliability, Execution Variance, and Institutional Risk Posture. |
| **What it asks** | 4 blocks of structural propositions. Evidence capture (5 fields) before escalation. |
| **What it produces** | A structural reading with band classification, pattern, first-action recommendation, and routing. |
| **What it remembers** | All scores, the band classification, evidence bridge responses, and checkpoint. |
| **What it unlocks** | Executive Reporting (when evidence warrants). Strategy Room. |
| **What it must never claim** | Independent institutional audit. Multi-respondent validation (unless team assessment preceded). Certainty about organisational state from one respondent. |
| **Evidence posture** | User-reported via dual-axis. System-computed bands. System-inferred patterns. Evidence bridge is user-reported. |

---

## Stage 06 — Executive Reporting (Paid)

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A paid, board-grade decision report that prices consequence, identifies governance corrections, and sequences interventions. |
| **What it asks** | Full intake: name, organisation, role, sector, authority scope, board involvement, revenue band, decision window, headcount, stakeholder breadth. Plus 5 high-yield free-text questions. |
| **What it produces** | Financial exposure estimates, constitutional posture, priority stack, failure modes, governed directives. |
| **What it remembers** | Everything. The full intake, the computed report, the evidence chain, checkpoint responses, and outcome verification. |
| **What it unlocks** | Strategy Room. Return Brief. Boardroom Mode (when qualified). Counsel (when warranted). |
| **What it must never claim** | Financial forecast. Audited figures. Independent validation. "Board-ready" unless the artifact genuinely supports board use. |
| **Evidence posture** | User-reported intake. System-computed exposure (estimated). ClaimGovernedCapabilities pattern controls disclosure. |

---

## Stage 07 — Strategy Room

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A governed execution environment. Locks a decision, sequences interventions, tracks decisions, schedules checkpoints, and monitors consequence. |
| **What it asks** | Execution inputs only — what is missing to begin. No repeated evidence collection. |
| **What it produces** | An active execution session with intervention stack, decision log, checkpoint schedule, consequence score, and irreversibility estimate. |
| **What it remembers** | Every decision made, every checkpoint response, every avoidance pattern, every consequence score. |
| **What it unlocks** | Return Brief. Boardroom Mode. Counsel escalation. Outcome verification. Retainer consideration. |
| **What it must never claim** | That the action is correct. Guaranteed outcomes. Independent verification of irreversibility. "The system has determined" — it has inferred. |
| **Evidence posture** | System-monitored execution. Decision log is user-reported. Irreversibility is system-estimated. Consequence score is system-computed. |

---

## Stage 08 — Return Brief

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | A confrontation document. Makes inaction visible. Compares commitments to reality. Checks prior standards. |
| **What it asks** | Checkpoint response: completed, partially completed, blocked, abandoned, or disputed. |
| **What it produces** | A private briefing showing trajectory, prior standard, consequence evidence, cost of inaction, and a direct challenge. |
| **What it remembers** | The checkpoint response as governance evidence. Changes the system's treatment of the case. |
| **What it unlocks** | Retainer trigger (if pattern persists). Counsel escalation (if evidence warrants). Outcome verification. |
| **What it must never claim** | Independently verified consequences. Financial precision. That the user "failed." |
| **Evidence posture** | GOLD STANDARD. Every block source-labelled. Consequence evidence marked "not independently verified." Cost of inaction labelled "estimated." |

## Escalation Surfaces

### Decision Centre
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | The case console. Shows all active Living Cases under governance. |
| **Must never claim** | Priority or score without supporting data. "Verified" recurrence without confirmation. |

### Counsel Room
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | Governed escalation to human counsel when evidence shows automated analysis should not proceed alone. |
| **Must never claim** | That counsel is "available for purchase." That the system "determined" counsel is needed. |

### Boardroom Mode
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | Board-grade dossier delivery, gated by evidence qualification. |
| **Must never claim** | "Verified outcomes" without specifying verification method. That boardroom is a premium tier. |

### Proof Pack
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | Durable evidence export showing what the system captured, what the user answered, what was reported, and what was reviewed. |
| **Must never claim** | Self-reported outcomes as verified. Evidence the system did not capture. |

### Oversight / Retainer
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What it is** | Retained decision enforcement with governed cycles, oversight briefs, and consequence tracking. |
| **Must never claim** | "Decision Authority as a Service." That oversight is optional when evidence shows persistence. |

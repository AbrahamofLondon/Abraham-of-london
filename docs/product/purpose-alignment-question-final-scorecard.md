# Purpose Alignment Question Final Scorecard

> Date: 2026-05-08
> Standard: Every question must score at least 8/10 across 10 dimensions.

---

## Scoring Dimensions

1. **Evidence yield** — Does the answer produce usable diagnostic signal?
2. **Emotional honesty** — Does it force the user to confront a real condition?
3. **Structural usefulness** — Does it serve downstream scoring, routing, or reporting?
4. **Specificity** — Does it constrain vague answers?
5. **Non-generic wording** — Would a competitor ever ask this?
6. **Scoring clarity** — Can the user place themselves on a 0–10 scale?
7. **User dignity** — Does it challenge without shaming?
8. **Downstream usefulness** — Does it feed contradiction detection, pattern matching, or narrative?
9. **Domain fit** — Does it belong in its assigned domain?
10. **Differentiation** — Does it make the product feel proprietary?

# Retainer Debt Closure Hostile Audit

**Date:** 2026-05-08  
**Intent:** Assess whether the retainer system is now operationally deliverable, reviewable, persistent, client-safe, and cycle-aware.

---

## Hard questions

### Is the system now retainer-grade?

Retainer-grade minimum: nearly.

The system can now generate, review, archive, compare, and present a client-safe oversight brief through a governed route. Delivery state is real enough to prepare and record controlled client view without pretending email or scheduling exists.

### Can a £5k client receive value safely?

Yes, if operated through the reviewed client-safe brief surface and internal bench.

The client can receive a governed cycle artifact with:

- executive summary
- structured actions
- cost movement where real
- recurrence where real
- irreversibility and option risk where real
- value protected
- cancellation-loss visibility
- next review window

### Can a £15k client receive value safely?

Partially.

The sponsor-safe and board-safe outputs now differ, but organisation-level divergence and richer multi-user oversight still need stronger Control Room infrastructure.

### Is £50k still premature?

Yes.

Enterprise Control Room, scheduler-backed cadence, richer role model, boardroom archive depth, counsel history, and cross-organisation retained intelligence are still incomplete.

### Can the brief be delivered?

Yes, through a governed client-view surface at `/oversight/brief/[cycleId]`.

This is delivery-capable, not email-automated. That is the correct minimum.

### Can the brief be reviewed?

Yes.

`/admin/oversight-review` now acts as an operator review bench rather than a raw preview utility.

### Can the brief be compared to a prior cycle?

Yes, when a prior archived cycle exists.

The comparison is now based on durable archived cycles rather than temporary preview state.

### Can counsel escalation be actioned?

Yes, at foundation level.

Counsel handoff can now be assigned and recorded as a governed workflow, but counsel evidence-node production is still procedural rather than deeply integrated.

### Can sponsor-safe and board-safe outputs differ?

Yes.

Audience-safe outputs now exist for:

- `CLIENT_SPONSOR`
- `BOARD_LEVEL`
- `RESPONDENT_SAFE`

### What would a client lose by cancelling?

They would lose durable visibility over:

- cost accumulation
- commitment drift
- recurrence detection
- irreversibility posture
- option decay
- dependency pressure
- boardroom threshold movement
- counsel-triggered governance escalation

### What remains ordinary?

- delivery is still portal-style, not a differentiated delivery ritual
- counsel workflow is governed but still thin
- no scheduler-backed cadence yet
- no enterprise oversight archive UX beyond the cycle artifact

### What remains dangerous to claim?

- “fully automated retainer delivery”
- “enterprise command centre”
- “institutional command” as an operational fact
- “cross-organisation intelligence memory” as a sellable feature
- any claim that delivery or scheduling is automatic

Counsel Workflow Grade: `FOUNDATION_ONLY`

£5k Readiness: `YES_WITH_OPERATOR_DISCIPLINE`

£15k Readiness: `PARTIAL`

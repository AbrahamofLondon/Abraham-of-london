# Retainer Operating Rhythm

Retainer oversight is a governed cycle, not a monthly report.

## Cycle

1. Internal brief is composed from real case, execution, recurrence, cost, boardroom, and counsel evidence.
2. Client-safe outputs are derived per audience boundary.
3. Operator reviews efficacy, suppressions, cadence, and delivery readiness.
4. Review decision is recorded.
5. Delivery state is moved only to a real internal lifecycle state.
6. Cycle archive is preserved for comparison, counsel memory, and boardroom memory.
7. Next-cycle intent is derived without pretending scheduler automation exists.

## Cadence

- `FIRST_CYCLE_PENDING` means no archived cycle exists yet.
- `REVIEW_DUE` / `REVIEW_OVERDUE` means the brief has not cleared governed review.
- `DELIVERY_DUE` / `DELIVERY_OVERDUE` means review is complete but delivery is not yet recorded.
- `CYCLE_MISSED` / `CADENCE_BROKEN` means the oversight institution is falling behind its own operating standard.

## Required Brief Questions

- What changed?
- What worsened?
- What repeated?
- What became more expensive?
- What became harder to reverse?
- What was ignored?
- What was verified?
- What required counsel?
- What required board attention?
- What would the client have missed without retained oversight?
- What must happen before the next cycle?

## Doctrine

Generation is mechanical.

Review is institutional.

Delivery is earned.

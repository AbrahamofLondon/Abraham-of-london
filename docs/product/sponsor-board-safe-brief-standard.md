# Sponsor / Board-Safe Brief Standard

## Audience modes

- `SPONSOR_SAFE`
- `BOARD_SAFE`
- `OPERATOR_INTERNAL`
- `RESPONDENT_SAFE`

## Sponsor-safe may show

- aggregate divergence
- cadence state
- unresolved commitments
- cost exposure
- recurrence
- boardroom readiness
- counsel status
- required sponsor action

## Sponsor-safe must not show

- raw respondent text
- named respondent identity from anonymous campaigns
- individual answers
- operator notes
- internal warnings
- hidden suppression rationale beyond safe explanation
- technical engine language

## Board-safe may show

- consequence
- unresolved decision
- exposure
- recommended board action
- objection handling
- certainty boundary

## Board-safe must not show

- operational respondent detail
- internal counsel notes
- granular campaign mechanics
- private operator annotations

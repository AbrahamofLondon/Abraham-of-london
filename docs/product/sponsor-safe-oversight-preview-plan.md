# Sponsor-Safe Oversight Preview Plan

**Date:** 2026-05-08
**Authority:** Decision Infrastructure by Abraham of London

## Purpose

Sponsor-safe oversight preview exists to deliver monthly governed intelligence without leaking respondent-level or operator-only material.

It is not a raw brief dump.

## Sponsor may see

- aggregate case state
- total cost of inaction where basis is real
- boardroom qualification counts
- counsel trigger status at aggregate level
- decision credit movement at aggregate level
- pattern recurrence where it does not identify individual respondents
- required actions framed at sponsor-safe level
- retained enforcement cycle totals where contract evidence exists

## Sponsor may not see

- raw respondent text
- anonymous respondent identity
- small-sample case breakdowns
- individual psychological or personality framing
- operator-only internal notes
- counsel-sensitive content until reviewed

## Suppression rules

- if sponsor cannot view named respondents, case labels become generic
- if sample threshold is too small, per-case detail is suppressed
- if a section depends on operator-only signal interpretation, the interpretation is removed rather than rewritten
- if aggregate visibility is not permitted, no client-safe brief is produced

## Delivery principle

The client-safe brief should preserve commercially useful intelligence while making suppression explicit.

Suggested copy:

Some respondent-level detail has been suppressed because this campaign does not meet the visibility threshold for sponsor review.

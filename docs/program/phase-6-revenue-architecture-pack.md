# Phase 6 Revenue Architecture Pack

Status: PLANNED
Live surface truth: This pack describes the intended commercial system. It should not be read as evidence that pricing, monetization, or paid flows are fully live.

## Objective

Turn the current funnel into a governed monetization system without diluting the brand or collapsing advisory quality.

The rule is simple:

- free surfaces create signal
- paid interpretation creates revenue
- private advisory captures high-consequence demand
- membership compounds retention and authority

## Canonical Product Ladder

| Layer | Canonical offer | Buyer state | Primary route / surface | Revenue role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Shorts and Essays | Problem-aware but not qualified | `/shorts`, `/blog` | Audience formation |
| 2 | Resources and Strategic Frameworks | Seeking structure | `/downloads`, `/resources/strategic-frameworks` | Lead capture and trust |
| 3 | Diagnostics | Actively seeking signal | `/diagnostics` | Qualification |
| 4 | Executive Reporting | Serious buyer needs interpretation | `/diagnostics/executive-reporting` | Flagship product revenue |
| 5 | Strategy Room | High-stakes decision with authority | `/strategy-room` | High-margin advisory conversion |
| 6 | Advisory Engagement | Decision made, execution requires guidance | post-Strategy Room | Retained or scoped revenue |
| 7 | Membership | Wants ongoing access, doctrine, and restricted assets | legacy `/inner-circle`, Vault surfaces | Recurring revenue and retention |

## Revenue Design Principles

### 1. Do not monetize noise

The system should only charge meaningfully after a user has:

- declared a meaningful problem
- demonstrated decision gravity
- shown authority or access to authority
- accepted the seriousness of the environment

### 2. Separate interpretation from intervention

`Executive Reporting` should remain the flagship interpretation product.

That preserves three advantages:

- the buyer can purchase clarity before buying change
- the system avoids premature advisory sales pressure
- the eventual Strategy Room is populated with better-qualified principals

### 3. Membership is a compounding layer, not the core close

Membership should not be the first serious commercial ask for high-consequence buyers.

It should function as:

- continuity for serious readers
- restricted doctrine and archive access
- a retention layer after value has already been proven

### 4. Strategy Room remains scarce

Strategy Room capacity is part of pricing logic.

Scarcity should be defended through:

- admission standards
- fit qualification
- authority screening
- explicit refusal criteria

## Recommended Commercial Architecture

### A. Public authority layer

Purpose:

- create demand
- educate the buyer
- build philosophical and strategic authority

Includes:

- Shorts
- Essays
- selected Resources
- selected Strategic Frameworks
- selected public diagnostics entry points

Commercial behavior:

- free to enter
- aggressively tagged and instrumented
- each asset points to Diagnostics or Executive Reporting

### B. Signal layer

Purpose:

- transform interest into measurable intent
- identify seriousness before advisory exposure

Includes:

- Diagnostics
- journey-state capture
- stage progression logic
- exit and drop-off analysis

Commercial behavior:

- mostly free or low-friction
- designed for qualification, not immediate margin
- primary CTA is Executive Reporting for qualified users

### C. Flagship paid interpretation layer

Purpose:

- monetize seriousness without overcommitting scarce advisory time

Primary offer:

- Executive Reporting

Deliverable shape:

- board-grade interpretation
- diagnosis and pattern reading
- decision implications
- recommended paths
- clear next-step logic

Commercial behavior:

- premium pricing
- framed as a decision-quality product, not a downloadable report
- becomes the hinge point between funnel and advisory

### D. Scarce advisory layer

Purpose:

- capture high-value principals whose stakes justify direct intervention

Primary offer:

- Strategy Room

Commercial behavior:

- qualified entry only
- tightly scripted intake
- no generic consultation positioning
- outcomes defined in advance

### E. Continuity layer

Purpose:

- increase lifetime value
- keep serious operators inside the ecosystem
- preserve access to doctrine, tools, and controlled archives

Primary offers:

- Membership
- Vault access
- periodic briefings
- framework updates

## Indicative Price Architecture

These are pricing bands for architecture planning, not live-code defaults.

| Offer | Positioning | Indicative band | Pricing logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Executive Reporting | Flagship interpretation | Premium fixed fee | High enough to pre-qualify seriousness, lower than bespoke advisory |
| Strategy Room | Scarce decision chamber | Premium session fee or paid admission | Reflects authority access, preparation, and consequence |
| Advisory Engagement | Bespoke intervention | Custom scoped | Derived from decision complexity, stakeholder count, and cadence |
| Membership | Continuity and restricted access | Recurring monthly or annual | Lower-friction retention layer, not principal flagship monetization |

## Qualification Logic by Stage

| Stage | Signal observed | Next best commercial move | Do not do |
|---|---|---|---|
| Content consumption only | Attention | Move to Diagnostics or gated Resource | Push Strategy Room |
| Diagnostic entry, low completion | Curiosity or low commitment | Retarget with sharper authority content | Sell advisory directly |
| Diagnostic completion, moderate seriousness | Emerging buyer | Offer Executive Reporting | Route straight to retained work |
| Executive Reporting interest with authority and urgency | Qualified principal | Offer Strategy Room | Dilute with generic membership pitch |
| Strategy Room accepted | High-consequence buyer | Offer scoped Advisory Engagement if justified | Force continuity product too early |
| Post-delivery loyal user | Proven trust | Offer Membership and Vault continuity | Restart buyer at top of funnel |

## Recommended Copy Discipline

Use these canonical frames:

- `Diagnostics` for qualification
- `Executive Reporting` for paid interpretation
- `Strategy Room` for private advisory
- `Membership` for recurring access
- `Vault` for restricted archive value

Avoid these weak frames:

- coaching
- discovery call
- consultation package
- premium content subscription

## Required Supporting Assets

To make this revenue architecture operational, the program needs:

- KPI sheet and funnel reporting spec
- offer-specific page copy recommendations
- objection handling language
- stage-based qualification heuristics
- decision rules for when not to sell

## Decisions Locked by This Pack

- Executive Reporting is the flagship monetization hinge
- Strategy Room is scarce and qualified
- Membership is retention and continuity, not the first serious close
- Diagnostics exists primarily to qualify, not merely to entertain
- Vault value should support continuity, not replace paid interpretation

# Phase 8-9 Authority Scaling Pack

Status: PLANNED
Live surface truth: Parts of the authority layer are live, but this pack describes the target authority engine and market-control model, not a completed rollout.

## Objective

Turn content, frameworks, and market language into compounding authority that feeds the funnel and strengthens market position.

Phase 8 is the authority engine.
Phase 9 is the move from authority to language control and distribution leverage.

## Authority System Model

Authority should flow in one direction:

`Shorts -> Essays -> Resources / Frameworks -> Diagnostics -> Executive Reporting -> Strategy Room`

That path creates:

- attention
- trust
- seriousness
- qualification
- monetization

## Content Role Map

| Content type | Primary role | Funnel job | CTA destination |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shorts | Capture attention and emotional recognition | Top-of-funnel entry | Essays, Diagnostics |
| Essays | Deepen worldview and frame the problem correctly | Mid-funnel authority | Diagnostics, Strategic Frameworks |
| Strategic Frameworks | Prove structured thinking | Qualification and trust | Executive Reporting, Strategy Room Intake |
| Resources | Provide usable tools | Lead capture and operational trust | Diagnostics, Membership |
| Canon | Establish doctrine and intellectual seriousness | Deep authority | Membership, Vault, Strategy Room pre-read |
| Vault Briefs | Restricted premium insight | Continuity and exclusivity | Membership, Advisory trust reinforcement |

## Content-to-Funnel Mapping

### Shorts

Best for:

- resonance
- self-recognition
- shareability
- language seeding

CTA rule:

- one step deeper only
- push toward Essays or Diagnostics

### Essays

Best for:

- reframing the buyer’s understanding
- demonstrating seriousness
- introducing canonical language

CTA rule:

- route toward Diagnostics, Strategic Frameworks, or Executive Reporting

### Strategic Frameworks

Best for:

- proving the system is operational, not rhetorical
- serving founders, operators, directors, and institution-builders

CTA rule:

- route to Executive Reporting or Strategy Room Intake

### Canon and Vault

Best for:

- depth
- continuity
- retained trust
- identity formation around the system

CTA rule:

- route toward Membership, Vault continuity, and pre-read depth for qualified principals

## Territory Map

The authority engine should consistently own five territories:

| Territory | What it means |
|---|---|
| Governance | How decisions are structured, owned, and reviewed |
| Strategic Clarity | How signal is separated from noise |
| Institutional Resilience | How systems survive pressure and drift |
| Leadership Formation | How leaders are shaped to carry consequence |
| Legacy and Stewardship | How responsibility scales over time |

## Editorial Calendar Logic

Use a 4-part cadence:

1. Recognition
   Shorts and Essays that name the buyer’s lived problem
2. Reframing
   Essays and Frameworks that explain the real issue
3. Operationalization
   Resources and Frameworks that make the thinking usable
4. Qualification
   CTAs into Diagnostics, Executive Reporting, or Strategy Room Intake

## Linking Policy

Every content asset should link in only one of three directions:

- deeper into doctrine
- sideways into operational tools
- forward into the funnel

Avoid dead-end content.

### Required linking patterns

| From | To |
|---|---|
| Shorts | Essays or Diagnostics |
| Essays | Diagnostics, Strategic Frameworks, Canon |
| Frameworks | Executive Reporting, Strategy Room Intake, Resources |
| Canon | Membership, Vault, selected Frameworks |
| Vault Briefs | Membership continuity, selected advisory-prep surfaces |

## Phase 9: Market Control Layer

Authority becomes market control when the system owns the language by which serious buyers describe their own problem.

## Framework Registry Rule

No new flagship framework name should be published until it is recorded in a controlled framework registry with:

- name
- definition
- promise
- misuse warning
- related artifacts
- canonical CTA

Suggested initial registry classes:

- Decision Hygiene
- Governance Ladder
- Pressure Protocol
- Institutional Design Kit
- Integrity Moat Model

## White Paper Roadmap

Use `Briefing Papers` as the program term.

Initial roadmap:

- infrastructure and institutional resilience

- doctrine and decision rights in modern institutions

## Distribution Map

- private salons and briefing rooms

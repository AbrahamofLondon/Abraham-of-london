## Positioning Discipline (All Waves)

Do not pitch as:
- A parenting manual
- A legal grievance
- A court memoir
- A trauma dump
- A religious testimony
- A revenge narrative

Pitch as:
> "A compressed literary memoir about fatherhood, inheritance, faith, and the cost of refusing to disappear."

The court material exists in the manuscript. It is not the book's centre. Do not make it the pitch's centre.

## Response Protocol

- **Full manuscript request:** Respond within 48 hours to confirm. Pause any pending queries in the same wave to avoid a conflict. Do not withdraw other queries — agents understand simultaneous submissions.
- **Pass with feedback:** Record the feedback in `10-submission-tracker.csv`. Assess whether the feedback warrants query revision before the next wave.
- **Pass without feedback (silence after 12 weeks):** Record as a pass. Do not follow up unless the agent has explicitly indicated a different response window.
- **No response after stated window:** Record as implied pass. Move on.

---

## What Success Looks Like

At the agent stage, success is a full-manuscript request, followed by an offer of representation. The book does not need universal enthusiasm — it needs one agent who responds with clarity and conviction. Adjust positioning between waves if feedback reveals a consistent misread.

If all three waves are exhausted without representation: reassess positioning, manuscript status, and market timing before a second round.

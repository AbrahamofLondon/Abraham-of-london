## 3. Levers Implemented

### Lever 1 — Close the covenant loop. **IMPLEMENTED.**

Added one restrained movement (~365 words gross) inserted after "It made the burden truer." and before "Love was not recklessness." It connects:
- **Ch.7** — the seven-year-old's bargain in the dark ("his life, kept… a price he was not allowed to name"; woke, put out a fire, lived; "not finished").

It bridges private/romantic fear to the wider structure of fear-as-punishment without naming any court or legal specific ("the fear of a man who has learned that to be afraid can itself be treated as a fault"). One clean deployment; no translation discussion; no sermon.

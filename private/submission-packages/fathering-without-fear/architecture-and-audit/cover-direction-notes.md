# Cover Direction Notes — Fathering Without Fear

Private file. Do not include in Wave 1 submissions.

This is not a commercial cover brief for the current agent package. Wave 1 should remain text-only, restrained, and submission-led. No mock book cover, artwork, stock image, AI-generated image, religious iconography, court imagery, child imagery, dramatic fire imagery, flags, skyline clichés, or motivational-memoir design should be inserted into agent-facing materials without explicit owner approval.

## Visual Standard

The visual identity should support this reading:

> A grave, compressed literary memoir about a father trying not to disappear.

It should not imply a parenting guide, legal campaign, custody memoir, trauma product, inspirational testimony, or commercial self-help brand.

## Future Concepts

### Concept 1 — The Empty Contact Room

A restrained cover built around absence: a chair, a table, a waiting room, or a quiet institutional space. No child shown.

Mood: controlled, intimate, unsentimental, literary.

### Concept 2 — The Northern Line

A father moving through London repeatedly, without melodrama. Could use line, route, or movement as abstraction.

Mood: urban, repetitive, faithful, quiet pressure.

### Concept 3 — The Fire and the Room

A childhood room, smoke, light, survival. Not dramatic flames. More memory than disaster.

Mood: origin story, spiritual pressure, survival.

### Concept 4 — Inheritance / Iyalode

A more ancestral direction: fabric, threshold, hands, road, or old Nigerian domestic texture. Avoid cliché African patterning.

Mood: lineage, memory, matriarchal force, faith and inheritance.

### Concept 5 — The Boy's Voice

The title held against the emotional core of father and son, without showing the child. A small object could carry this: football boots, a number 7 shirt detail, a schoolbag, or a folded card.

Mood: tenderness, restraint, fatherhood without exploitation.

## Type and Palette

Suitable directions:

- Times New Roman, Garamond, Georgia, or similarly restrained serif typography
- black, charcoal, warm off-white, muted grey
- generous white space
- no decorative framing

Avoid:

- red, gold, bright blue
- religious stock imagery
- courtroom or gavel imagery
- handcuffs or custody symbolism
- crying child imagery
- London skyline clichés
- Nigerian flag literalism
- flames as spectacle

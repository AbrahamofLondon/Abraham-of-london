# Submission Notes — Nicola Chang, David Higham Associates

---

## Why Nicola Chang

Chang is among the most aesthetically specific agents on the UK literary nonfiction list. She has named Janet Malcolm and Annie Ernaux as writers she responds to — both defined by compression, moral seriousness, and the refusal to make their subject matter legible through conventional narrative comfort. *Fathering Without Fear* operates inside the same aesthetic: it withholds legal detail, uses short chapters as formal pressure chambers, and refuses the arc of resolution that a grievance narrative would require.

Her stated interest in "the boundary between fiction and nonfiction" is directly relevant to this manuscript. The book is prose memoir that moves with the precision of literary fiction. She is not a genre-category agent; she is an aesthetic-category agent. The pitch must match her aesthetic register, not her subject-matter checklist.

She is at David Higham Associates, one of the UK's leading agencies, with strong commercial and rights infrastructure.

---

## What This Agent Responds To

- Formal compression: chapters that carry weight without over-explaining
- Voice that is controlled, specific, not performative
- Moral seriousness — the refusal to sentimentalise
- Work that operates at the limit of what can be told
- Power, class, and cultural intersection through a personal lens
- The Ernaux influence: the way private experience reveals social structure without ever becoming polemical

---

## What to Emphasise in Your Interactions

If she requests more materials or has questions, emphasise:

1. **The formal architecture**: each chapter is a closed unit; the compression is the form, not a production limitation
2. **What the book withholds and why**: it does not narrate the legal proceedings because the child inside the story is alive; protecting him is a formal as much as an ethical choice
3. **The Iyalode thread**: the grandmother who carried both Christian faith and Yoruba spiritual knowledge without perceiving them as contradictions — this is the book's deepest philosophical strand
4. **The father's death and what it costs**: the way Abraham sat with his father's body at the wake and did not fully feel it until later is precisely the kind of compression that Ernaux and Malcolm do

---

## What NOT to Lead With

- Do not mention family court proceedings in any detail
- Do not describe the book as "about the legal system" — it moves through the legal system; it is not about it
- Do not frame it as a victim narrative or an injustice story
- Do not over-explain the Yoruba cultural content — let it speak through the prose
- Do not mention other agents you have queried

---

## Agency Logistics

- **DHA submission rule:** One agent at a time. Do not query Veronique Baxter, Andrew Gordon, Jessica Woollard, Jemima Forrester, or any other DHA agent while Chang is active. If Chang passes, you may then query another DHA agent.
- **Response window:** DHA does not publish a standard response time. Allow 12 weeks before treating as an implied pass.
- **Follow-up:** No follow-up before 12 weeks. After 12 weeks, a single brief email checking status is acceptable.
- **Word document requirement:** Non-negotiable at DHA. Any PDF submission will be ignored or rejected. Ensure the Word file is properly formatted before sending.

---

## Positioning Reminder

This book should reach Chang as:

> "A compressed literary memoir about fatherhood, inheritance, faith, and the cost of refusing to disappear — written in the aesthetic tradition of Annie Ernaux and Janet Malcolm."

Not as: a court story, a grievance, a parenting book, or a faith testimony.

---

## Risk Assessment

**Upside:** Chang's aesthetic profile is as close a match as exists on the UK market. If the prose quality holds at the level the best chapters demonstrate, this is a strong submission.

**Risk:** She is primarily a fiction agent who crosses into literary nonfiction selectively. Her nonfiction list is shorter than her fiction list. The submission needs to clear her prose bar, not just her subject-interest bar.

**Mitigant:** Lead with Ch.1 (Hounslow Call) and Ch.6 (The Fire and the Deal) — these are the book's most formally compressed chapters and the ones most likely to demonstrate the aesthetic she looks for in the first 50 pages.

**Alternative if Ch.1 is too short for the form's minimum word requirement:** Add **Chapter 6 — The Fire and the Deal** (2,506 words) as a second chapter, for a combined total of approximately 4,047 words. This demonstrates both the institutional voice (Ch.1) and the spiritual/ancestral voice (Ch.6).

# Submission Notes — Sarah Levitt, Aevitas Creative Management

---

## Why Sarah Levitt

Levitt has articulated a specific category — "memoir plus" — that this book inhabits precisely. She describes it as work that is not quite memoir, not quite straight nonfiction, but something that fuses a personal story with a larger architecture. *Fathering Without Fear* is exactly that: it is memoir plus inheritance study, plus spiritual reckoning, plus multigenerational history, plus institutional endurance — and the 24-chapter compressed structure is its formal architecture. No single label captures the book, and Levitt has built her list around the books that don't fit clean labels.

She has represented MacArthur Genius grant recipients and has a reputation for culturally significant nonfiction — writing that matters beyond the individual story. Her note that she is "typically closed" but welcomes "extraordinary voices" signals a high bar that this manuscript is built to clear.

Aevitas Creative Management is one of the leading US literary agencies, with strong institutional positioning and transatlantic rights capacity.

---

## What This Agent Responds To

- "Memoir plus": the book that exceeds its genre category; personal story as entry point into something larger
- Formal architecture: the sense that the author has made deliberate structural choices, not simply recounted events
- Culturally significant nonfiction: work that addresses questions that extend beyond the personal — inheritance, fatherhood, race, faith, institutional power
- Underrepresented voices — Nigerian-British, Black British father, diaspora experience not yet well-represented in the US memoir market
- Literary quality: this is not a "platform book" and Levitt's list reflects that; she wants the writing to be as good as the story

---

## What to Emphasise in Interactions

If Levitt responds or requests more:

1. **The formal architecture** — not a memoir in the confessional mode; 24 pressure-chamber chapters, each carrying a single weight; the structure was chosen, not inherited from the events
2. **The "plus" elements** — what the personal story opens onto: the ancestral Isua lineage as colonial and post-colonial history; the Iyalode figure as a study in how spiritual knowledge is transmitted across generations; the contact rooms as a study of what institutional structures do to fathers
3. **The cultural significance** — no book currently occupies the position of a Black British father writing about fatherhood, ancestral inheritance, and institutional endurance with literary seriousness. This is a gap, not a crowded category.
4. **The Psalm 27:10 through-line** — "When my mother and father forsake me, then the Lord will take me up." This verse runs through the book from the father's prayer in Lagos to the narrator's fatherhood in London. It is the book's theological spine, understood not as doctrine but as inheritance.

---

## What NOT to Lead With

- Do not describe the book as "memoir" without immediately expanding the category — Levitt will not respond to straight memoir; she responds to "memoir plus"
- Do not describe it as a grievance or a legal narrative — the institutional material is present but it is the lens, not the subject
- Do not pitch faith content as devotional — the faith in this book is ancestral, tested, and specific; it has passed through a woman who lived to 118 and a father who prayed from Psalm 27; it is not evangelical
- Do not mention private grievance in any form
- Do not name other Aevitas agents under any circumstances

## Positioning Reminder

This book should reach Sarah Levitt as:

> "'Memoir plus' in the most exact sense: a personal story fused with inheritance study, spiritual reckoning, and institutional critique — compressed into 24 chapters that refuse to let any single register dominate. Culturally significant, formally deliberate, and written from inside a life that has not yet been written about at this level."

## Risk Assessment

**Upside:** Levitt's "memoir plus" framing is the most precise description available of what this book is. She has the literary sensibility and the institutional standing to place this book at the level it deserves. Her MacArthur Genius client history signals she works at the highest level of nonfiction.

**Risk:** She is "typically closed" — her bar is very high. The query must earn its way through in the first paragraph alone, with precision, not enthusiasm. A generic submission would not reach her. The query in this packet is written specifically for her register.

**Mitigant:** The "memoir plus" opening paragraph signals that the author understands her list before the pitch begins. This is the most important aspect of the personalisation for Levitt — she needs to know that the author's understanding of the work matches her own before she invests in the pitch.

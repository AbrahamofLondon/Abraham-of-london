## Structure
- **Repair aftermath opening:** the chapter opens with the roof holding overnight, but the floorboard near the no-colour door making a new sound. The first pressure is practical: dry pot, marked floor, damp cloths, board memory, rent book on the shelf.
- **Practical repair remains spiritually unresolved:** the roof holds, the room remains closed, the stain remains, the board clicks, and Brother Paul's wedges become a practical object that keeps the floor from opening without healing the house.
- **Obianuju's visible cost:** she guards her wrist, moves carefully, pays Brother Paul's bill herself, refuses to let sympathy make the protagonist central, and returns with wedges that extend the repair into another task. She remains active and exact.

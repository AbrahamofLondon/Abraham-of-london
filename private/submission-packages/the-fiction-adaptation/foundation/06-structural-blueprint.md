# Structural Blueprint — Full Length

**Purpose:** The 30-chapter skeleton at depth, integrating the force architecture, the motif system, the register palette, and **per-chapter word targets to full literary length.** This supersedes the provisional first-pass calibration (the lean Movement I drafts will be expanded to these targets through *scene*, not padding).

**Total target:** ~90,000 words (88k–94k band). Three movements. Chapter lengths vary by spiritual weight (world bible §2), not evenly.

| Movement | Chapters | Target words | Dominant force |
| :------- | :------- | -----------: | :------------- |
| I — The Current | 1–10 | ~29,000 | Spirit Husband (Claim) |
| II — The Cost | 11–21 | ~35,000 | The Voice (Intimidation) |
| III — Outliving, Not Winning | 22–30 | ~26,000 | The Trance (Warning) |

Legend — **Reg.** = register (1 grave-reflective · 2 tender-domestic · 3 uncanny-plain · 4 lyric-liminal/trance · 5 plain-narrative). **Img.** = dominant motif (see `04`).

## Calibration notes

- **Length:** average ~2,900/chapter → ~88k–94k. The drafted Ch.1–3 (~1,400–1,750) will be **expanded to target** by deepening scene — more of the town's embodied life, more of Obianuju's ordinary presence, more incident — never by adding cosmology or commentary (voice bible §8).
- **Register modulation is mandatory per chapter** (no chapter in one register the whole way). The Reg. column lists the dominant blends; the plain/tender registers are the relief valves that keep gravity from becoming monotony.
- **Motif discipline:** one dominant image per chapter (Img. column), transformed from its prior appearances (`04`).
- **Duality audit:** every chapter has a named duality beat. At each movement's close, audit that no chapter shows what Obianuju costs him without a nearby chapter showing what was true and good in her.
- **The tense-bleed accelerates** across the columns: trance-present is contained in I, leaks in II (Chs 12, 21), and is uncontainable in III — the reader's map failing on schedule (world bible §2).

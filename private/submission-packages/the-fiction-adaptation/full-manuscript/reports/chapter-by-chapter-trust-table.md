## Whole-Book Chapter Necessity Finding

No chapter is structurally disposable. The manuscript's chapter logic is cumulative:

- Chapters 1-5 establish the private contract and sample hook.
- Chapters 6-10 widen witness, history, and metaphysical mechanism.
- Chapters 11-17 reverse the protagonist's role from rescuer to risk.
- Chapters 18-23 test obedience through practical domestic pressures.
- Chapters 24-28 convert private mystery into public story, then resolve through chosen boundary.

The chapters with the highest line-watch risk are 27 and 28, not because they lack function, but because the final argument is so clear that some reflective restatement becomes optional.

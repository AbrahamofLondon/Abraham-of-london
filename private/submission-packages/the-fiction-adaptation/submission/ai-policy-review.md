## Manuscript Development Disclosure

### AI-Assisted Editorial Workflow

*The Fiction Adaptation* has been developed with AI-assisted editorial tools in the following stages:

1. **Cadence auditing and pattern analysis:** AI-assisted identification of repeated phrases
2. **Line-edit execution:** AI-assisted removal of weak instances of patterns ("for the first time," "not because...but because...")
3. **Manuscript assembly:** AI-assisted reassembly of canonical chapters into full-manuscript files
4. **Query letter development:** AI-assisted drafting and refinement
5. **Synopsis creation:** AI-assisted outline and structure
6. **Positioning documentation:** AI-assisted framing and market analysis
7. **Agent research:** AI-assisted agent targeting list development

### What Was NOT AI-Generated

- Original manuscript prose (author-written)
- Manuscript decisions (author-made)
- Editorial direction (author-directed)
- Final acceptance of all edits (author-approved)
- Query letter content and claims (author-verified)
- All creative work and narrative vision (author-authored)

### What Was AI-Assisted

- Pattern identification and counting
- Editorial workflow management and tracking
- Manuscript assembly and file management
- Market research and agent list compilation
- Document formatting and organization
- Administrative and coordination tasks
